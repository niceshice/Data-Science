Last chance to apply for European Parliament Journalism Prize
Culture
2008-05-05 - 17:21
Calling all EU journalists…
Are you a registered journalist, resident in a European Union country? Did you publish an article or report on major EU issues between 1 May 2007 and 30 April 2008? If the answer to those questions is yes, don’t miss the chance to apply for the new European Parliament Prize for Journalism.
The prize was launched this year to reward journalists who have contributed to a better understanding of the EU and the way it works. It will be awarded in October, with winners in the categories of: printed press, internet, radio and television. Each winner will receive €5000.
What do you have do?
You must be resident in the EU and a registered journalist. You can register and participate individually or as part of a team of five or less people. How? By sending in your articles or reports.
The articles and reports can be "one-off" pieces or part of a series. They must deal with EU-related issues or be about EU institutions and policies and they must have been published or appeared between 1 May 2007-30 April 2008. Entries that have received a prize, or which received EU funding, are not eligible.
Check the offical website
An application form and complete set of rules can be found on the official website of the European Parliament Prize for Journalism, click on the link below.
The deadline for applications is 15 May 2008.
