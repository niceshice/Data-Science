A woman exits a booth to cast her vote for the European parliamentary elections at a polling station on June 7 in Strasbourg, France. Sunday sees electors from 19 nations including heavyweights France, Germany, Italy and Spain vote. AFP/ F.FLORIN People cast their ballot at a polling station in Umbrete, south west Spain on June 7, 2009 during the European parliamentary election. AFP PHOTO / C. Quicler An elderly women votes in Riga, Latvia on June 6.Latvia's government was confronted with its first ballot test since the Baltic state fell victim to the economic crisis AFP Izontins. A Bulgarian voter leaves the polling booth after casting her ballot in Sofia on June 7, 2009. Around 6.7 million Bulgarians are eligible to vote. AFP B. Katsarova. An elderly woman casts her ballot for the European Parliament vote at a polling station in Bratislava. The turnout in 2004 was the lowest in Europe - at 17%. AFP S.Kubani. Local election ballot papers are sorted and counted at the Turf Moor leisure centre in Burnley, northwest England, on June 5, 2009. Europe's Super election Sunday will see voters in 19 countries head to the polls to elect Members of the European Parliament. Here is a snapshot of some of the images from around Europe as people went to the polls. Since Thursday people across the continent have been choosing who they want to represent them for the next 5 years. Amid an economic downturn there is uncertainty about the political direction of the European Union. All of the results from the results will be available Sunday night.
