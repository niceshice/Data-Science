The Week Ahead - 19 to 25 June 2006
Institutions
2006-06-16 - 10:45
Special plenary session and committee meetings - Brussels
Special plenary session. Austrian Chancellor and Council President-in-Office Wolfgang Schüssel reports to Parliament on the outcome of the Brussels summit of 15-16 June (Tuesday) . Turkey. Enlargement Commissioner Olli Rehn addresses the Foreign Affairs Committee following the start of the first chapter of Turkey’s accession negotiations and ahead of next month’s vote by MEPs on a progress report on Turkey (Tuesday) . Albania. Following the signing of a pre-accession agreement between Albania and the EU, Prime Minister Sali Berisha appears before the Foreign Affairs Committee (Thursday) . ECB. Jean-Claude Trichet, President of the European Central Bank, delivers his customary review of the eurozone economy to a meeting of the Economic Affairs Committee (Wednesday) . EU-ACP. EP President Josep Borrell attends the 11th session of the EU-ACP Joint Parliamentary Assembly, which is being held in Vienna from 17 to 22 June. Air quality. The Environment Committee votes on two reports, one on ambient air quality and the other on the thematic strategy on air pollution (Wednesday) . Car tax. The Economic Affairs Committee votes on a report on taxation of private cars, the aim of which is to take better account of the realities of the single market and of environmental sustainability (Wednesday) . Equitable Life. The Committee of Inquiry into the crisis of the Equitable Life society will hear evidence from the British and Irish financial services regulators (Wednesday). Structural Funds. The Regional Development Committee votes at second reading on a package of reports on the Structural Funds' multiannual programmes (Thursday) .
