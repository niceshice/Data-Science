European Parliament President Jerzy Buzek held a meeting with Czech President Václav Klaus in Prague on Friday morning. The two Presidents discussed the ratification of the Lisbon Treaty in the Czech Republic.
Mr Buzek said: "The meeting with President Klaus was held in a very open and frank manner. President Klaus stated that the Czech Republic would like to have the same or similar arrangements on the Charter of Fundamental Rights as the United Kingdom and Poland. After the meeting, I am optimistic and will work to find a solution.
This afternoon, I will have further meetings with Czech political leaders. Tomorrow in Warsaw, I will meet with Prime Minister Fredrik Reinfeldt and President José Manuel Barroso, when Poland will complete its ratification of the Lisbon Treaty, to discuss the latest developments."
