Juncker calls for ambition, enthusiasm and vision
Economic and monetary affairs
Plenary sittings
2011-09-27 - 17:32
John SCHRANZ BXL (+32) 2 28 44264 STR (+33) 3 881 74076 PORT (+32) 498 98 14 02 EMAIL econ-press@europarl.europa.eu On Tuesday afternoon MEPs and Euro Group President Jean-Claude Juncker debated the best setup for strengthening Euro zone governance and how best to resolve the Greek crisis. Mr Juncker reiterated that it was time for more ambitious structures, not based on the intergovernmental method, and declared himself totally against any country leaving the Euro zone. He also called for this generation of European politicians to show enthusiasm and vision and refrain from populism.
