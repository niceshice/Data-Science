The Week Ahead 26 January - 1 February 2009
2009-01-23 - 10:16
Committee meetings and political groups week – Brussels
President’s diary. EP President Hans-Gert Pöttering will chair a meeting of the Bureau of the Euro-Mediterranean Parliamentary Assembly, with the Gaza conflict high on the agenda (Thursday). He will then travel to Prague for a meeting with the Speakers of the Czech, French, Spanish and Swedish Parliaments (Friday) .
Fight against terrorism. The Security and Defence Subcommittee and the Civil Liberties Committee will hear from the EU’s antiterrorism coordinator, Gilles de Kerchove, and from Interpol representatives, on progress made in combating terrorism (Thursday).
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament’s political groups devote most of the week to preparations for the 2-5 February plenary session in Strasbourg. Among the main points on the agenda are a debate and vote on the final report from Parliament’s temporary Committee on Climate Change, legislation to penalise employers of illegal immigrants, an overview of energy strategy, a resolution on the EU’s future role in Kosovo and rules on the labelling of animal feed. There is also scheduled to be a formal address by Palestinian President Mahmoud Abbas.
Pre-session briefing. Any changes made to the plenary agenda by the Conference of Presidents will be announced at the regular pre-session briefing with Parliament’s press service and the spokespeople of the main political groups (Friday 11am Anna Politkovskaya room PHS 0A050).
