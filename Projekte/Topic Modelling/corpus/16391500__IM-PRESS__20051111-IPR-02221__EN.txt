Result of votes at November I plenary session
Institutions
2005-11-17 - 18:27
A list of the all the votes at the November I plenary session in Strasbourg. A selection of more detailed press releases is available on Parliament's Press Service website.
Results of votes on Thursday 17 November
REACH
Report on the proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on the Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals (REACH), establishing a European Chemicals Agency and amending Directive 1999/45/EC and Regulation (EC) No …/… [on Persistent Organic Pollutants]
(A6-0315/2005)
Rapporteur Guido SACCONI (PES, IT)
Report adopted by 407 votes in favour to 155 against with 41 abstentions, with amendments.
Adaptation to REACH
Report on the proposal for a directive of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Council Directive 67/548/EEC in order to adapt it to Regulation (EC) No .../... of the European Parliament and of the Council concerning the registration, evaluation, authorisation and restriction of chemicals
(A6-0285/2005)
Rapporteur Guido SACCONI (PES, IT)
Report adopted with amendments.
Mobilisation of the Solidarity Fund
Report on the proposal for a decision of the European Parliament and of the Council on the mobilisation of the European Union Solidarity Fund according to point 3 of the Interinstitutional Agreement of 7 November 2002 between the European Parliament, the Council and the Commission on the financing of the European Union Solidarity Fund supplementing the Interinstitutional Agreement of 6 May 1999 on budgetary discipline and improvement of the budgetary procedure
(A6-0320/2005)
Rapporteur Reimer BÖGE (EPP-ED, DE)
Report adopted.
Draft amending Budget No 7/2005

(A6-0321/2005)

Rapporteur Karin SCHEELE (PES, AT)

Results of votes on Wednesday 16 November
Black list of air carriers
Report on the proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on the information to air transport passengers on the identity of the operating carrier and on communication of safety information by Member States
(A6-0310/2005)
Rapporteur Christine DE VEYRAC (EPP-ED, FR)
Report adopted by 577 votes in favour to 16 against with 31 abstentions. An agreement was reached with the Council on the amendments voted by Parliament, which means that the regulation will be adopted in this form.
Slovak power plant Bohunice VI
Report on the proposal for a Council regulation on the implementation of Protocol No 9 on the Bohunice V1 nuclear power plant in Slovakia, as annexed to the Act concerning the conditions of accession to the European Union of the Czech Republic, Estonia, Cyprus, Latvia, Lithuania, Hungary, Malta, Poland, Slovenia and Slovakia
(A6-0282/2005)
Rapporteur Rebecca HARMS (Greens/EFA, DE)
Report adopted without changes by 377 votes in favour to 222 against with 59 abstentions.
