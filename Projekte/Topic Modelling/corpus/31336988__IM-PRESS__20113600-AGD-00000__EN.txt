The Week Ahead 29 August - 4 September 2011
2011-08-26 - 10:09
Committee meetings - Brussels
Situation in the Eurozone. The Economics Committee will discuss the sovereign debt markets with the President of the ECB, Jean-Claude Trichet, the President of the Eurogroup, Jean-Claude Juncker, Polish Minister of Finance, Jacek Rostowski, and Commissioner for Economic and Monetary Affairs, Olli Rehn (Monday p.m). On Wednesday the committee will debate its draft report on the ECB's activities for 2010 and the way forward for the Bank (Wednesday p.m.) .
Mortgage credits. New legislation for residential mortgages in Europe will also be debated by Members of the Economics Committee. The legislation aims inter alia to reduce the build-up of excessive risk while guaranteeing better access to mortgage markets (Wednesday p.m).
Palestinian Authority. The International Trade committee will vote on whether or not to give its consent to a trade agreement between the EU and the Palestinian Authority providing further liberalisation of trade in agricultural and fisheries products (Wednesday a.m.).
Inauguration of "Simone Veil Agora" and "Solidarność 1980 Esplanade". President Jerzy Buzek will inaugurate the "Simone Veil Agora" and the "Solidarność 1980 Esplanade", parts of the promenade at the European Parliaments in Brussels. Simone Veil, former President of the European Parliament, Lech Walesa, former President of the Polish Republic and first leader of the Solidarność movement, Donald Tusk, Polish Prime Minister and Yves Leterme, Belgian Prime Minister will be among those taking part in the official ceremony and in the unveiling of the commemorative plaques (Tuesday, 12.00).
Briefing on parliamentary agenda. A briefing of the parliamentary agenda is planned as usual in committee weeks on Monday at 11.00 (Room Anna Politkovskaya).

Trafficking of Human Beings - presentation by Myria VASSILIADOU, EU Anti-Trafficking Coordinator 17:45 18:30 Brussels, PHS 1A2 Website of the Committee on Women's Rights and Gender Equality Watch webstreaming live Catch up the video on demand (VOD) Other events Workshop: Cyber Security in Europe The workshop, organised by the Policy Department for Economic and Scientific Policy, will deal with questions of cyber threats, networks' security and proper response to cyber attacks and security breaches. Following the opening remarks by Mr Giles CHICHESTER (ECR, UK), member of the Industry, Research and Energy Committee and rapporteur for legislation on European Network and Information Security Agency (ENISA), participants will discuss the issues in three sessions. The first session will focus on plans to establish a computer emergency response team for EU institutions. The second session will analyse cyber threats for European public entities and pan-European cyber exercises, while the third will deal with concrete cyber threats for the European industry. Participants will be mostly cyber security experts from different member states who will share their experience and participate in debate with members of the Committee.
