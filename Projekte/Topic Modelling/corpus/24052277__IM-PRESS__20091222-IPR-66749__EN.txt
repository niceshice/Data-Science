Press service contacts over Christmas and New Year
Institutions
2009-12-22 - 16:15
The European Parliament is in recess over the Christmas and New Year holidays.
Parliament's offices are closed from 23 December 2009 to 3 January 2010 inclusive. Parliamentary business resumes on 11 January with a week devoted to hearings of Commissioners-designate and meetings of political groups ahead of the 18-21 January plenary session.
Urgent press enquiries during the holiday period should be addressed to:
23-27 December: Malene Chaucheprat on +32 498 983 325 28 December - 3 January: Marjory van den Broeke on +32 498 983 586
Parliament's Press Service wishes its readers a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.
