50th anniversary of EU - celebrations in Ireland
As a country which has benefited greatly from membership of the European Union, Ireland will be celebrating the EU's 50th birthday in many ways and in many different places this weekend. The European Parliament Office in Ireland has been preparing for these celebrations during the past few weeks and its activities will involve school children, journalists and members of the general public.
Prizewinners of schools competition participating in EUROSCOLA
On 25 March, the winners of a national public-speaking competition , organised by Ireland's National Forum on Europe with the involvement of the Irish MEPs will participate in a special Euroscola event in the European Parliament in Strasbourg. The Irish prizewinners from Holy Child School in Killiney will join with students from the 26 other EU Member States to discuss the future of the EU and a number of topical issues such as climate change, education and culture, and transport
New look website for the EP Office in Ireland
On Friday 23 March, the European Parliament Office in Ireland will launch its 'new look' website to mark the 50 th anniversary of the EU. The new website aims to make the European Parliament and its activities more accessible to Irish citizens. Along with information about the role of the European Parliament, its structures, the Irish MEPs, etc., the website will include a news section which will be updated regularly.
Special radio programme on the 50 th anniversary
One of Ireland's newest national radio stations, Newstalk, will broadcast a special three hour programme on Sunday, 25 March to celebrate the 50 th anniversary. The presenter and her team will spend the preceding Thursday and Friday in the European Parliament in Brussels speaking to MEPs, Commissioners, Irish journalists in Brussels and representatives of the Irish community in Brussels. The rest of the programme will then be broadcast live from their studios in Dublin on Sunday.
Head of the European Parliament Office participates in Third Level debate
On Thursday 22 March , the Head of the European Parliament Office in Ireland will participate in a special seminar in Dublin's Institute of Technology on 'EU Policy Communication'. Other members of the panel will include former Green MEP, Patricia McKenna and Alan Dukes, Director of the Institute of European Affairs. It is expected that the debate will be lively and that audience participation will be high.
Special Ecumenical Service on Sunday to celebrate 50 th anniversary
Irish MEP Eoin Ryan (FF/UEN) and Head of the EP Office in Ireland, Francis Jacobs will attend a special ecumenical service in St Patrick's Cathedral in Dublin on Sunday, 25 March to celebrate the 50 th anniversary. Irish Internal Market Commissioner, Charlie McCreevy will present an EU flag during the service.
