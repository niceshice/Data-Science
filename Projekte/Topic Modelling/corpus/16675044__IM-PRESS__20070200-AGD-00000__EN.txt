The Week Ahead 8-14 January 2007
2007-01-05 - 12:03
Political groups week - Brussels
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament's political groups will devote most of the week to preparations for the Strasbourg plenary session of 15-18 January. This session will see the formal welcoming of the new MEPs from Bulgaria and Romania. The agenda begins with Josep Borrell's final address as EP President and continues with the election of his successor. Other key items include the presentation by Chancellor Angela Merkel of the programme of the German presidency of the Council and the second reading debate on a package of legislation on Europe's railways.
Pre-session press briefing. Any changes to the agenda adopted by the Conference of Presidents will be announced at the regular pre-session briefing at 11am on Friday 12 January.
