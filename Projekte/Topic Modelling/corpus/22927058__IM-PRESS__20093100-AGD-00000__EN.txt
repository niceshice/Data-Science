The Week Ahead 27 July - 2 August 2009
2009-07-24 - 11:16
Parliament in recess
The European Parliament is in recess. Business resumes on 24 August with a week of meetings of political groups followed from 31 August by a week of meetings of parliamentary committees.
General Press enquires during this period should be made, in the first instance, to the general press service number

Elections in Moldova. A delegation of MEPs will be in Moldova to observe the Parliamentary elections of 29 July (Monday-Thursday).
2009-07-27 Other events MEPs observe parliamentary elections in Moldova Seven MEPs will be in Moldova to observe the new legislative elections due on Wednesday 29 July. Parliament's observation mission will be led by Romanian MEP Marian-Jean Marinescu (EPP). On the eve of the poll, the MEPs will meet the main political parties competing in the election, which is a re-run of the election held on 5 April. The EP delegation will also meet media representatives and will consult the head of the OSCE Parliamentary Assembly observation mission, the Greek parliamentarian Petros Efthymiou.
The EP delegation will hold a press conference in Chisinau on Thursday morning (date and time to be confirmed).
Together with Marian-Jean Marinescu, the delegation is made up of Andrzej Grzyb (EPP, PL), Cristian Dan Preda (EPP, RO), Zigmantas Balčytis (S&D, LT), Kristian Vigenin (S&D, BG), Renate Weber (ALDE, RO) and Tatjana Zdanoka (Greens/EFA, LV). 09:00 19:00 Chisinau (Moldova) 2009-07-28 Other events MEPs observe parliamentary elections in Moldova Seven MEPs will be in Moldova to observe the new legislative elections due on Wednesday 29 July.
