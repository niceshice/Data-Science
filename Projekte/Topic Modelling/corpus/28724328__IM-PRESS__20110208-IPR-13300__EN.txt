MEPs sceptical about Franco-German "competitiveness pact"
European Council
General
2011-02-08 - 11:42
European Council President Herman Van Rompuy was at the European Parliament on Tuesday. He reported on the conclusions of last weekend's EU summit and discussed with MEPs the situation in Egypt, energy and innovation, but above all economic recovery and the Franco-German proposal for a "competitiveness pact".
