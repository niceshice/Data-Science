The Week Ahead 7-13 December 2009
Institutions
2009-12-04 - 11:36
Committee meetings and political groups week – Brussels
European Council . EP President Jerzy Buzek will address EU leaders at the start of the final EU summit of 2009, after which he will hold a press conference (Thursday, Justus Lipsius building). Mr Buzek also has a meeting with the new President of the European Council, Herman Van Rompuy (Friday).
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament’s political groups will devote most of the week to preparations for the 14-17 December plenary session in Strasbourg. The main points of the agenda include the final vote on the 2010 EU budget and the award ceremony for the 2009 EP Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought to Oleg Orlov, Sergei Kovalev and Lyudmila Alexeyeva, on behalf of Memorial and all other human rights defenders in Russia. There will be a debate with Swedish Prime Minister Fredrik Reinfeldt on the outcome of Sweden’s EU presidency.
ECB/Jean-Claude Trichet. The President of the European Central Bank will take part in the regular Monetary Dialogue with the Economics Committee (Monday).
Swedish Presidency. The Swedish Foreign Minister, Carl Bildt, will discuss the achievements of the Swedish presidency with the Foreign Affairs Committee (Thursday), while the Finance Minister, Anders Borg, and the Financial Markets Minister, Mats Odell, appear before the Economics Committee (Monday).
Climate change – island states. Apisai Ielemia, Prime Minister of Tuvalu, will discuss the impact of climate change on small island developing nations with the Development Committee (Thursday).
EP President/Speakers’ Conference. President Buzek will be in Stockholm for a meeting of the Speakers of Parliaments of the European Union (Friday).
Pre-session press briefing. Any changes to the plenary agenda adopted by the Conference of Presidents will be announced at the regular briefing, with Parliament’s press service and the political groups’ spokespeople on hand to comment on the main points of the session (Friday 11am, Anna Politkovskaya room).
