The Week Ahead 18-24 September 2006

2006-09-15 - 10:46
Political groups week - Brussels
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament's political groups will devote most of the week to preparing for the Strasbourg plenary session of 25-28 September. The main points on the agenda include a debate on the EU's area of freedom, security and justice, as well as reports on Turkey's progress towards EU accession, air quality and services of general interest. Romanian Prime Minister. Calin Popescu-Tariceanu, Prime Minister of Romania, will meet EP President Josep Borrell and the leaders of Parliament's political groups. He will also hold a joint press conference with Mr Borrell. (Thursday) Moldovan Parliamentary speaker. Mr Borrell will also be meeting Marian Lupu, Speaker of the Parliament of the Republic of Moldova. (Wednesday) Euro-Med - Lebanon. A delegation from the Euro-Mediterranean Parliamentary Assembly, including three MEPs, will travel to Lebanon to assess the situation there after the recent conflict with Israel. (Sunday to Friday) Citizens' Forums. The first in a new round of Citizens' Forums on the future of Europe, organised by Parliament as part of the 'period of reflection', take place in Rokycany (Wednesday) and Horazdovice (Friday) in the Czech Republic. Pre-session press briefing. Any changes to the plenary agenda adopted by the Conference of Presidents will be announced at the regular press briefing at 11am on Friday.
