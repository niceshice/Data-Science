Constituency Week
Official visit to Croatia and FYROM. President Josep Borrell makes a four-day visit to Croatia and the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia, where he will meet the presidents and prime ministers of both countries. (Monday to Thursday). Fourth citizens' forum. As part of the debate on the future of Europe and the period of reflection following the rejection of the constitution in France and the Netherlands, the European Parliament is holding its fourth citizens' forum in Lublin, Poland (Monday) . Parliament's offices will be closed from Thursday 13 April to Monday 17 April. Work resumes after the Easter break on 18 April.
