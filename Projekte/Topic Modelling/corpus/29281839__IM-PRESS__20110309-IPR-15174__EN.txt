Parliament approves plenary session dates for 2012 and 2013
Institutions
Plenary sittings
2011-03-09 - 13:01
MEPs on Wednesday adopted the schedule of Parliament's plenary sessions for the years 2012 and 2013.
MEPs also adopted, by secret ballot, amendments to the proposed calendars such that two sessions in Strasbourg will now be held during a single calendar week in October 2012 and October 2013. The amendments were adopted by 357 in favour, 255 against and 41 abstentions (2012 calendar) and 356 in favour, 253 against and 35 abstentions (2013 calendar) respectively.
