Parliament backs Belgian candidate Peter Praet for ECB board
Economic and monetary affairs
Plenary sittings
2011-03-24 - 12:37
Peter Praet, Executive Director of Belgium's Central Bank, won Parliament's approval Thursday to take a seat on the Executive Board of the European Central Bank. The vote follows the Economic and Monetary Affairs Committee's recommendation at a hearing last week.
Mr Praet, whose candidature was approved with 527 votes in favour, 37 against and 71 abstentions, will replace Austrian Gertrude Tumpel-Gugerell, whose term on the ECB Executive Board comes to an end in May.
