Calendar of part-sessions 2009 - European elections 4-7 June 2009
Institutions
2008-05-21 - 13:26
The European Parliament adopted the calendar of part-sessions for 2009. European elections will take place from 4-7 June 2009. In 2009, eleven part-sessions will take place in Strasbourg and four two-day part-sessions will take place in Brussels. Calendar can be accessed via the link hereunder.
Part-sessions : 4-day sessions take place in Strasbourg (Monday PM to Thursday PM)
2-day sessions take place in Brussels (Wednesday PM to Thursday AM)
European elections : 4-7 June 2009.
