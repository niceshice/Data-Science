The Week Ahead 2 - 8 November 2009
Institutions
2009-10-30 - 12:31
Committee meetings - Brussels
Telecoms package conciliation . Internet access will be the main issue when Parliament's delegation and the Council will meet to seek an agreement on the telecom package. (Wednesday evening)
President's activities. President Buzek will hold a keynote speech at the Bundestag, for the Commemoration ceremony on the occasion of the 20th anniversary of the fall of the Berlin wall (Sunday). During the week he will also meet the president of Estonia (Thursday), the speaker of the Icelandic Parliament (Wednesday), and UEFA president Michel Platini (Thursday).
US Security Secretary visit. US Secretary of Homeland Security Janet Napolitano will meet Parliament's US delegation and the Civil Liberties Committee, followed by a press conference (Friday).
Discharge for the Council. The Budgetary Control Committee will vote for a second time on whether to grant the Council a discharge for its financial management in 2007 (Wednesday).
Climate Change. The Environment Committee and Commissioner Dimas will discuss latest developments in the climate change negotiations ahead of the UN summit in Copenhagen in December (Wednesday).
