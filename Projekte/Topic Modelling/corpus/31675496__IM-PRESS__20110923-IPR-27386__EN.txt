Free Food Scheme: MEPs call for swift action to ensure proper funding
Social policy
Plenary sittings
2011-09-28 - 10:04
Ján JAKUBOV BXL (+32) 2 28 32119 STR (+33) 3 881 74005 PORT (+32) 498 98 35 90 EMAIL fish-press@europarl.europa.eu The Council and Commission must take prompt action to avert a sharp cutback in funding of the Free Food scheme, an EU programme for the needy with more than 13 million beneficiaries, said MEPs across the board in today's debate with Commissioner Dacian Cioloş and Polish Minister Marek Sawicki.
MEPs also denounced the six Member States which are blocking debate in the Council on ways to save the scheme and asked the Polish Presidency to help to end the impasse.
