2008-01-07 - 12:42
Committee meetings and political groups week - Brussels
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament's political groups will devote most of the week to preparation for the plenary session of 14-17 January in Strasbourg. Among the main points on the agenda are the presentation by Slovenian Prime Minister Janez Janša of the programme of his country's presidency of the Council, a second-reading vote on the consumer credit directive and first reading votes on legislation on airport charges and on reducing greenhouse gas emissions from fuels. Parliament will also be addressed by the Grand Mufti of Syria, the first of a number of religious leaders invited to speak during the European Year of Intercultural Dialogue.
EP President visits Slovenia. Hans Gert-Pöttering will make an official visit to Slovenia on Tuesday, including meetings with the President, Prime Minister and the Speaker of the National Assembly. He will also take part in the launching ceremony for the Year of Intercultural Dialogue.
Pre-session press briefing. Changes to the plenary agenda adopted by the Conference of Presidents will be announced at the regular press briefing at 11am on Friday (Room PHS 0A050). The EP Press Service and representatives of the main political groups will be on hand to comment on the agenda.
