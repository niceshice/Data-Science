"Europe is the answer": Donald Tusk presents Poland's EU priorities
Institutions
Plenary sittings
2011-07-06 - 17:20
"The answer to the crisis is more Europe," Polish Prime Minister Donald Tusk said, presenting his country's Presidency programme to the EP on Wednesday. In a speech centred on Europe's common values and future, Mr Tusk strongly defended European integration and solidarity "within and between member states". The Polish Presidency of the EU Council runs from 1 July to the end of December.
