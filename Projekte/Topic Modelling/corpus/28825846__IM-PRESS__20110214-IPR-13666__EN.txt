EU asylum system: MEPs to debate with Commissioner Malmström
Asylum policy
Plenary sittings
2011-02-15 - 09:59
On Tuesday afternoon Parliament will discuss with Commissioner Cecilia Malmström the state of the EU asylum system in the light of a recent ruling by the European Court of Human Rights. MEPs are expected to ask about the implications of the ruling for the Dublin II system, which determines which Member State is responsible for dealing with an asylum application.
You can watch the debate via EP Live and EbS+ (links below).
