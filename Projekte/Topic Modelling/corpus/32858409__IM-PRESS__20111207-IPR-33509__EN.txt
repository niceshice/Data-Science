The leaders of the political groups and the President of the European Parliament will have an exchange of views on the proposals to be discussed by the Heads of State and Government and on the main messages to be delivered by President Buzek, during his discussion with the EU Heads of State on Friday.
The meeting of the Conference of Presidents will begin at 08:30 and is expected to end at 10.00.
