The Week Ahead 15-21 June 2009
2009-06-12 - 10:10
European Council – Brussels
EU Summit. Heads of State or Government of the European Union meeting in Brussels on Thursday and Friday are expected to decide on who to propose to Parliament as the next President of the European Commission. European Parliament President Hans-Gert Pöttering will address EU leaders at the start of the meeting and then hold a press conference (Thursday, venue: Council Press Room, Justus Lipsius building, 1800tbc).
Transition to the new Parliament. Informal meetings will continue among newly elected MEPs on the reorganisation of political groups in the light of the election results and the new requirement that a group needs at least 25 MEPs from at least seven countries. The outgoing Bureau of Parliament will also be meeting to deal with organisational matters in the run up to the inaugural session of the new Parliament on 14-16 July in Strasbourg.
