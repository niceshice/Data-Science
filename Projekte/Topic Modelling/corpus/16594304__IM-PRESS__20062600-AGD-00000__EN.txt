The Week Ahead 26 June - 2 July 2006
2006-06-28 - 13:57
Political groups week - Brussels
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament's political groups will devote most of the week to preparing for the Strasbourg plenary session of 3-6 July 2006. The session agenda includes a debate with the incoming Finnish presidency and votes on the structural funds and the interim report of the temporary committee on the alleged use of European countries by the CIA for the transport and illegal detention of prisoners.
Borrell in Guatemala and Denmark. EP President Josep Borrell will travel to Guatemala City to take part in the first World Summit of regional integration parliaments (Monday-Tuesday) and to Copenhagen to join the Conference of Speakers of EU national parliaments. (Thursday-Friday).
President of Haiti. René Préval, President of the Republic of Haiti, will join a meeting of Parliament's delegation to the ACP-EU Joint Parliamentary Assembly. (Wednesday)
Western Balkans cooperation. MEPs and parliamentarians from Albania, the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia, Serbia and Montenegro will be taking part in a meeting on the EU contribution to regional reconciliation and cooperation in the western Balkans. (Monday-Tuesday)
Pre-session press briefing. Any changes to the plenary agenda decided by the Conference of Presidents will be announced at the press briefing at 11am on Friday 30 June in Brussels.
