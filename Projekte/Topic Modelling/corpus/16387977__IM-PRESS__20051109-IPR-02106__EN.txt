Results of votes at October II plenary session
Institutions
2005-11-09 - 10:20
A list of the all the votes at the October II plenary session. A selection of more detailed analyses of votes is available on Parliament's Press Service website.
RESULTS OF VOTES ON THURSDAY 27 OCTOBER
Budget 2006
Draft general budget of the European Union - Financial year 2006
- Draft amendments to and proposals for modification relating to the appropriations in Section III of the 2006 draft general budget, concerning the Commission
- Draft amendments to Sections I, II, IV, V, VI, VII and VIII of the 2006 draft general budget, concerning the European Parliament, Council, Court of Justice, Court of Auditors, Economic and Social Committee, Committee of the Regions, European Ombudsman and European Data Protection Supervisor
Amendments to the Council budget proposal adopted by Parliament in first reading.
Parliament's vote results in a total budget for 2006 of 121.4 billion euros in commitments and 115.4 billion euros in payments.
Report on the draft general budget of the European Union for the financial year 2006; Commission
(A6-0309/2005)
Rapporteur: Giovanni Pittella (PES, IT)
Report adopted by 462 votes in favour to 74 against with 34 abstentions.
Report on the draft general budget of the European Union for the financial year 2006; other institutions
(A6-0307/2005)
Rapporteur: Valdis Dombrovskis (EPP-ED, LV)
Report adopted with some changes.
