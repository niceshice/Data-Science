Election countdown: 9 EU countries in 1979
2009 elections
Institutions
2009-05-19 - 09:07
The 2009 European elections will be the 7th election in 30 years When the European Parliament was first directly elected in 1979, voters of nine EU (then EEC) countries went to the polls. Today, nearly 500 million Europeans in 27 countries have a stake in the European elections.
The nine countries were France, Germany, Italy, Belgium, the Netherlands, Luxembourg, Ireland, the UK and Denmark.
