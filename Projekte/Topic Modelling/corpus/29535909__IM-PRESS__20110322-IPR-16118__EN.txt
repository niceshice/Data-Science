Debate on aftermath of disasters in Japan
Energy
Plenary sittings
2011-03-24 - 12:21
Following the earthquake and tsunami in Japan which caused thousands of deaths, and the serious accident at the Fukushima power plant with the consequent radioactive fallout, questions have been asked about the security of nuclear power plants in Europe. In a debate on Wednesday MEPs expressed their solidarity with the Japanese people and discussed the lessons Europe might learn from the disaster.
