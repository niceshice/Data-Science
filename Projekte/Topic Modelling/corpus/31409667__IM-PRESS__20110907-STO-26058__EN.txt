EP goes mobile - check it out!
Information
2011-09-08 - 13:13
The European Parliament is "mobile" For those who like to keep up with the Parliament, the good news is that it is now easier than ever following the launch of the new mobile website. Bringing together news and details of meetings, plenary sessions and committees, the mobile site provides the same distinctive content already available on the europarl website for those on the move.
The site has been developed to meet the needs of the ever-increasing number of people using smart phones and is adapted to use by mobile devices including iPhones, Blackberries and android mobiles. The new site is easy to use with user-friendly access to reports, news and MEPs. Click below to check it out.
Among the key features:
in 22 languages
breaking news, updated throughout the day
plenary agenda
information on committees
EP organisation
access to MEPs files via a research engine
meetings search engine
