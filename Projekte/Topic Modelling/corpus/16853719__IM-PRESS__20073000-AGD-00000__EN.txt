The week ahead 23 to 29 July 2007
2007-07-20 - 10:19
Opening of the Intergovernmental Conference - Brussels
IGC . The European Parliament will be participating in the Intergovernmental Conference (IGC) launch ceremony, which takes place on Monday 23 July at 12.15h, in the margins of the General Affairs and External Relations Council being held on Monday and Tuesday. Parliament's IGC representatives Elmar Brok ( EPP -DE, DE), Enrique Barón Crespo (P ES , ES) and Andrew Duff (A LDE , UK), will be present . A press conference with Council President Luis Amado, Commission President José Manuel Barroso and Elmar Brok for the EP will be held at the Council ( Justus Lipsius building ) , after the ceremony ( around 12.45h ).
