Extraordinary Committee meeting
Situation in Georgia. MEPs of the Foreign Affairs Committee, the Security and Defence Subcommittee and the Delegation for Relations with the South Caucasus will meet Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs Eka Tkeshelashvili to debate the current situation in Georgia. A number of press conferences and a press points on Georgia are scheduled ( Wednesday ).
