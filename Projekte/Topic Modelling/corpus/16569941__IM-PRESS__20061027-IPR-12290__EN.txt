2006-10-27 - 10:42
Constituency Week
Josep Borrell in Greece. The President of the European Parliament will make an official visit to Greece, during which he will meet the President, Prime Minister, Foreign Minister and the Speaker of the Greek Parliament. He will also address a meeting of three parliamentary committees and hold a press conference . (Monday and Tuesday)
Electoral observation - Congo and Nicaragua. An EP delegation will be in the Democratic Republic of Congo to observe the second round of the presidential elections, which takes place on 29 October. Another delegation will travel to Nicaragua to observe the presidential and legislative elections of 5 November.
MEPs in their constituencies. The week beginning 30 October is set aside for MEPs to work in their constituencies, so there are no official meetings of parliamentary bodies in Brussels. Parliament's offices will be closed from Wednesday to Friday for the All Saints holiday.
