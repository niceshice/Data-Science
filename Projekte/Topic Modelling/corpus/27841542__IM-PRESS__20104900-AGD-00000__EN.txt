The Week Ahead 29 November - 5 December 2010
2010-12-02 - 10:40
Committee meetings – Brussels
Citizens' initiative. The Constitutional Affairs Committee will vote on legislation setting up the citizens' initiative system (Tuesday).
People trafficking and protecting women from abuse. The Civil Liberties and Women's Rights committees jointly vote on a law to combat human trafficking and separate legislation aimed at giving cross-border protection to individuals at risk of gender-based violence (Monday).
Serbia's EU prospects. The Foreign Affairs Committee will vote on the EU -Serbia stabilisation and association agreement and also on a resolution assessing the country's EU integration progress (Wednesday).
ECB – Jean-Claude Trichet. The ECB President takes part in his regular dialogue with the Economics Committee (Tuesday).
EP President - Netherlands PM. EP President Jerzy Buzek will meet Dutch Prime Minister Mark Rutte in The Hague (Tuesday).
EU Roma strategy. The Civil Liberties Committee holds a public hearing on the EU strategy for Roma inclusion (Tuesday).
Family life and EU law. MEPs and national MPs will consider how to remove legal and administrative barriers in matters of family law with a cross-border aspect (Tuesday).
EU budget. The Budget committee meets to discuss the new proposal for the 2011 EU budget presented by the European Commission (Wednesday).
European environment. EP President Jerzy Buzek will join a press conference on the European Environment Agency's 2010 report on the EU environment with the agency's head, Jacqueline McGlade, Environment Commissioner Janez Potočnik and Joke Schauvliege, Flemish environment minister (Belgian Presidency) (Tuesday).
Conference of Presidents - Hungarian presidency. President Buzek and the political group leaders will be in Budapest to meet the future Hungarian Council presidency (Thursday - Friday).
ACP-EU assembly. The 20th session of the joint assembly which brings together MEPs with MPs from African, Caribbean and Pacific countries takes place in Kinshasa (Thursday – Saturday).
