Statement by President Pöttering on the situation of Bronislaw Geremek
MEPs: immunity and statute
2007-04-26 - 12:36
Just before Thursday's voting session, Parliament's President Hans-Gert Pöttering made an announcement on the situation of Polish MEP Bronislaw Geremek (ALDE). Mr Pöttering told the House that the EP's Conference of Presidents had approved, by a large majority although not unanimously, the press release issued by himself on this subject (see below). The Conference of Presidents had also "stated its sympathy and solidarity with our colleague Bronislaw Geremek".
