Report of European Environment Agency
Environment
2005-11-29 - 14:57
At the invitation of the EP Environment Committee, the European Environment Agency unveiled its five-year report to MEPs and journalists in Brussels on Tuesday.
The 570-page report reviews the current state of the environment and the future outlook. Although available only in English, the report is accompanied by summaries in all official EU languages and also includes sections on individual countries. These documents, together with a press release, can be read on the EEA's website : www.eea.europa.eu
2005-11-29 Chair of EP Environment Committee Karl-Heinz Florenz EPP-ED DE
