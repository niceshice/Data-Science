Christmas and New Year Parliamentary Recess
2006-12-22 - 09:47
The European Parliament is in recess over the Christmas and New Year Holidays.
Parliament's offices re-open on Wednesday 3 January 2007. Normal parliamentary business resumes on Monday 8 January, with a week of meetings of Parliament's political groups ahead of the Strasbourg plenary session of 15-18 January.
