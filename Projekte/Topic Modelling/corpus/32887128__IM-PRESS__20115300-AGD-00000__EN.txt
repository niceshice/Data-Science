Christmas and New Year Recess
2011-12-21 - 18:00
The European Parliament is in recess over the Christmas and New Year holidays.
Parliament's offices close from 22 December until 3 January. Business resumes on 9 January, with a week of meetings of parliamentary committees and political groups to prepare for the 16-19 January plenary session.
Urgent press enquiries during the holiday period should be addressed to:
22 December - 30 December: Michaela Findeis, +32 498 98 33 32 michaela.findeis@europarl.europa.eu
31 December - 3 January: Andreas Kleiner, +32 498 98 33 22 andreas.kleiner@europarl.europa.eu
Parliament's Press Service wishes its readers a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.
