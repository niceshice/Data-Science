Barroso and MEPs: no dilution of economic governance
Economic and monetary affairs
Plenary sittings
2011-03-08 - 18:19
In a debate with Commission President Barroso on Tuesday afternoon, both MEPs and the Commission warned Member States to work at EU level on any plans aimed at enhancing economic coordination. Intergovernmental agreements would not produce the much-needed results, argued MEPs.
