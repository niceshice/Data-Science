MFF: 2014-2020 budget freeze not possible unless agreed policies are slashed
Budget
Plenary sittings
2011-06-07 - 17:49
MEPs will debate the EU's next long-term budget (2014-2020) on Wednesday at 09h00. Parliament will adopt its formal opinion in a vote between 12h00 and 13h00.
The European Parliament is the first EU institution to take a stance on the financial perspective. The Commission will table its proposal on 29 June.
You can watch the plenary debate via EP Live, EbS+, and Europarl TV (links below).
