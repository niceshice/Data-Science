Committee meetings and political groups week - Brussels
Parliamentary business resumes. Following the summer break, MEPs return to Brussels for a series of committee meetings and the usual meetings of political groups to prepare for the Strasbourg plenary session of 3-6 September. Though changes are possible, the main topics of the draft agenda include the counter terrorism measures, natural disasters, transport of dangerous goods, the issue of liquids on board airliners and controls on the possession and purchase of weapons.
