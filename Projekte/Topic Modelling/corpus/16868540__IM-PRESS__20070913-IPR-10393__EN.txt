The Week Ahead 17-23 September 2007
Institutions
2007-09-13 - 18:27
Political groups week - Brussels
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament’s political groups will devote most of the week to preparations for the Strasbourg plenary session of 24-27 September. Among the main points on the agenda are: reports on immigration, the third-reading agreement on the third railway package, reform of the sugar market and the prospects for a common foreign policy on energy. There will also be a statement from the Commission on dangerous toys imported from China.
EP/Africa. EP President Hans-Gert Pöttering will meet Gertrude Mongela, President of the Pan-African Parliament. (Wednesday)
Citizens’ forums. The latest in Parliament’s meetings enabling members of the public directly to join the debate on the future of Europe take place in Bremen (Monday), Enschede (Wednesday), Barcelona (Thursday) and Vlissingen (Saturday).
Pre-session press briefing. Any changes to the plenary agenda adopted by the Conference of Presidents will be announced at the regular pre-session briefing at 11am on Friday (room PHS 0A050). The EP spokesperson and representatives of the main political groups will be on hand to comment on the agenda.
