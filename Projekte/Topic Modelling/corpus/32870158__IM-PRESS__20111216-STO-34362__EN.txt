Euro symbol on a statue outside EP in Brussels Â©Belga/Xinhua The working group tasked with drawing up a new agreement to tackle the economic crisis and reinforce economic union, as agreed at the summit 8 December, began its work Monday. On Tuesday evening the MEPs involved will discuss the initial results with members of the economic and constitutional affairs committees.
The EP delegation to the working group includes: German Christian Democrat Elmar Brok, Italian Socialist Roberto Gualtieri and Belgian Liberal Guy Verhofstadt. French Green Daniel Cohn-Bendit is a substitute member.
On 8 and 9 December EU leaders agreed on measures to tackle the crisis and secure the euro's future. The aim is to sign an agreement by March. It will apply to euro zone countries but non-euro zone member states may also ratify it. The UK is not taking part in the process.
