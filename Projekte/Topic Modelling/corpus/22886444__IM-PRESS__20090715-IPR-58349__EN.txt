Parliamentary committees complete elections of chairs and vice-chairs
Institutions
2009-07-20 - 20:05
The inaugural meetings of the parliamentary committees were completed on Monday with the eight remaining committees and the two subcommittees electing their chairs and vice-chairs.
Here is the full list of chairs and vice-chairs of the European Parliament's committees for the next two-and-a-half years:
Foreign Affairs Chair: Gabriele Albertini (EPP, Italy)
Vice-Chairs: Fiorello Provera (EFD, Italy) Ioan Mircea Paşcu (S&D, Romania) Dominique Baudis (EPP, France) Jean-Luc Mélenchon (GUE/NGL, France)
Development Chair: Eva Joly (Greens / EFA, France) Vice-chairs: Michèle Striffler (EPP, France) Nirj Deva (ECR, UK ) Iva Zanicchi (EPP, Italy) Corina Cretu (S&D, Romania)
International Trade Chair: Vital Moreira (S&D, Portugal)
Vice-chairs: Cristiana Muscardini (EPP, Italy) Robert Sturdy (ECR, UK ) Pawel Ksawery Zalewski (EPP, Poland) Yannick Jadot (Greens/EFA, France)
Budgets Chair: Alain Lamassoure (EPP, France)
Vice-chairs: Jutta Haug (S&D, Germany) Alexander Alvaro (ALDE, Germany) Jean-Luc Dehaene (EPP, Belgium) Ivaylo Kalfin (S&D, Bulgaria)
Budgetary Control Chair: Luigi de Magistris (ALDE, Italy)
Vice-chairs: Bart Staes (Greens/EFA, Belgium) Jean-Pierre Audy (EPP, France) Boguslaw Liberadzki (S&D, Poland) Tamás Deutsch (EPP, Hungary)
Economic and Monetary Affairs Chair: Sharon Bowles (ALDE, United Kingdom)
Vice-chairs: José Manuel Garcia-Margallo y Marfil (EPP, Spain) Arlene McCarthy (S&D, United Kingdom) Theodor Dumitru Stolojan (EPP, Romania) Edward Scicluna (S&D, Malta)
