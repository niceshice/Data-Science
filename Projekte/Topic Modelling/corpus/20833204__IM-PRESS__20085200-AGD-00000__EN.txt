The Week Ahead 22-28 December 2008
2008-12-19 - 10:33
Christmas and New Year Recess
EP/Gulf States. EP President Hans-Gert Pöttering continues his official visits to the Gulf states. He will meet the Head of State, government officials and parliamentarians first in Bahrain (Monday) , then in Saudi Arabia (Tuesday) .
Parliament is in recess over the Christmas and New Year holidays. Normal business resumes on Monday 5 January with a week of meetings of Parliament’s political groups to prepare for the 12-15 January plenary session.
Parliament’s Press Service wishes all its readers a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.
