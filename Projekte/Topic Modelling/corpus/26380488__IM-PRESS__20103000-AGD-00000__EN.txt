The Week Ahead 19-25 July 2010
2010-07-16 - 09:24
Parliament in recess
President's activities. EP President Jerzy Buzek joins the presidents of other EU institutions for a meeting with European religious leaders to discuss combating poverty and social exclusion. Mr Buzek, European Council President Herman van Rompuy and Commission President José Manuel Barroso will hold a joint press conference after the meeting (Monday, 12.15, Berlaymont press room ). The same day, President Buzek will have bilateral meetings with Mr Van Rompuy and with Mercedes Bresso, President of the EU Committee of the Regions.
Parliament is in recess. Business resumes on 30 August with a week of meetings of parliamentary committees and political groups ahead of the 6-9 September plenary session.
