Council to present its position on EU's 2012 budget
Budget
Plenary sittings
2011-09-13 - 17:00
Ron KORVER BXL (+32) 2 28 44659 STR (+33) 3 881 74903 PORT (+32) 498 98 35 88 EMAIL budg-press@europarl.europa.eu EMAIL ronnie.korver@europarl.europa.eu The Council will present its position on the EU budget for 2012 on Wednesday 14 September between 09.00h and 11.20h. A majority of Member States wants to limit any increase in the budget to 2.9% for commitments and 2.02% for payments, whereas the Commission has proposed 3.7% and 4.9% respectively. Parliament will vote its position on 26 October, after which there will be a 21-day conciliation period, starting on 1 November.
