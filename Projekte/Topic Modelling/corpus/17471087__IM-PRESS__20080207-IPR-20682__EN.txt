Joint Parliamentary Meeting on the Lisbon Strategy to be held in the European Parliament
Future of Europe/European integration
2008-02-08 - 10:11
The Fourth Joint Parliamentary Meeting (JPM) on the Lisbon Strategy, co-organised by the European Parliament and the Slovenian National Assembly will take place in Brussels on 11 and 12 February 2008. As in the three previous editions, this Joint Parliamentary Meeting will congregate Members of the National Parliaments and the European Parliament to discuss on different topics related to the Lisbon Strategy before the Spring European Council takes place.
The main topics on discussion in the Working Groups of this JPM will be the following: Internal Market and Strategies-Research and Development: Responses to Globalisation; Investment in People and Labour Market, and Measuring Progress and Monitoring Lisbon.
The Joint Parliamentary Meeting will be co-chaired by the President of the Slovenian National Assembly, Mr France Cukjati , and the Vice-president of the European Parliament, Ms Rodi Kratsa-Tsagarapoulou , on behalf of the EP President.
UK MEPs participating include:
Giles Chichester (EPP-ED, UK), John Bowis (EPP-ED, UK), Philip Bushill-Matthews (EPP-ED, UK), Malcolm Harbour (EPP-ED, UK)
Attending from the UK and Ireland:
Michael Connarty MP (Labour) - Chairman of the House of Commons European Scrutiny Committee
Lord Julian Grenfell - Chairman of the House of Lords Select Committee on the European Union
Lord Patrick Wright - Member of the House of Lords Select Committee on the European Union
Bernard Durkin - Member of the House of Oireachtas - Fine Gael
Deirdre de Burca - Member of the House of Oireachtas - Green party
