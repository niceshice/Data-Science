Campaign for 4-7 June European Elections
Election campaign. This week and the two following are set aside for MEPs to join their political parties and other candidates in the campaign for support in the European Parliament elections of 4-7 June 2009. There are therefore no official meetings planned in Brussels.
Open Days. Parliament opens its doors to the public on Saturday 9 May in Brussels.
