Watch live online: Hearing on future of public broadcasters
Culture
2009-03-05 - 09:02
A man with a television camera What is the role of public broadcasters in the new audiovisual landscape and what strategies should they focus on in future? Those questions will be discussed on Thursday when the European Parliament's Culture Committee hosts a public hearing on the future of public broadcasters, with the participation of MEPs, academics and experts from commercial TV and the public sector. Watch live from 1000-1230 CET.
The hearing "comes in a time of important evolution and changes in the regulatory framework concerning telecommunications and of new emerging innovatory technologies and services in the sector of audiovisual services," Chair of the Culture Committee. Katerina Batzeli told us ahead of the meeting.
The hearing is intended to allow "a more concrete clarification" of "their public mission and the means they use to reach it."
