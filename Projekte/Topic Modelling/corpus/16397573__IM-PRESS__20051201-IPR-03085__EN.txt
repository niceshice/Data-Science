Results of votes at plenary session 30 November-1 December
Institutions
2005-12-01 - 14:01
A list of the reports put to the vote in the European Parliament on Thursday 1 December. A selection of more detailed individual press releases is available from Parliament's Press Service website.
Financing instrument for development cooperation Proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council establishing a financing instrument for development cooperation and economic cooperation (A6-0060/2005) Rapporteur: Gay MITCHELL (EPP-DE, IE)
The report was referred back to the Development Committee.
EC-South Africa Agreement Proposal for a Council decision concluding the additional protocol to the Agreement on Trade, Development and Cooperation between the European Community and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of South Africa, of the other part, to take account of the accession of the Czech Republic, the Republic of Estonia, the Republic of Cyprus, the Republic of Latvia, the Republic of Lithuania, the Republic of Hungary, the Republic of Malta, the Republic of Poland, the Republic of Slovenia, and the Slovak Republic to the European Union (A6-0328/2005) Rapporteur: Miguel Angel MARTINEZ MARTINEZ (PES,ES)
Report adopted by raise of hands.
Euroregions Report on the role of "Euroregions" in the development of regional (A6-0311/2005) Rapporteur: KYRIACOS TRIANTAPHYLLIDES (GEU/NGL, EL)
Report adopted by raise of hands.
Immunity of Andrzej Pęczak Request for defence of the immunity and privileges of Andrzej Pęczak, former Member of the European Parliament (A6-0330/2005) Report Maria BERGER (PES, AT)
Report adopted by raise of hands, which means that Parliament will not defend Mr Pęczak's immunity.
Immunity of Giovanni Claudio Fava Request for the defence of the immunity and privileges of Giovanni Claudio Fava(PES, IT) (A6-331/2005) Report Klaus-Heiner LEHNE (EPP-DE, DE)
Report adopted by raise of hands, which means that Parliament will defend Mr Fava's immunity.
Posts at Civil Service Court
DAB no6/2005 section IV - Court of Justice (A6-0336/2005) Rapporteur: Anne Elisabeth JENSEN (ALDE, DK)
Report adopped by raise of hands.
Financial Perspective
Interinstitutional agreement on budgetary discipline and improvement of the budgetary procedure (2005/2237/INI)
(A6-0356/2005)
Rapporteur Reimer Böge (EPP-ED, D)
Report adopted by raise of hands.
Medicines for developing countries Proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on compulsory licensing of patents relating to the manufacture of pharmaceutical products for export to countries with public health problems (A6-242/2005) Rapporteur: Johan VAN HECKE (ALDE, BE)
Rapport adopted by 543 votes in favour to 21 against with 35 abstentions, enabling a compromise in first reading with the Council and Commission.
Bird flu Proposal for a Council directive on Community measures for the control of avian influenza (A6-327/2005) Rapporteur: Neil PARISH (EPP-ED, UK)
Report adopted with some amendments by raise of hands.
Veterinary expenditure Proposal for a Council decision amending Council Decision 90/424/EEC on expenditure in the veterinary field (A6-326/2005) Rapporteur: Ilda FIGUEIREDO (GEU/NGL, PT)
Report adopted by raise of hands.
Widening the Eurozone Proposal for a Council regulation amending Regulation (EC) n°974/98 on the introduction of the euro (A6-329/2005) Rapporteur: Dariusz ROSATI (PES, PL)
Report adopted by raise of hands
Common VAT system Proposal for a Council directive amending Directive 77/388/EEC on the common system of value added tax, with regard to the length of time during which the minimum standard rate is to be applied (A6-0323/2005) Rapporteur: Zsolt Laszlo BECSEY (EPP-ED, HU)
Report adopted by raise of hands.
VAT refunds Proposal for a Council directive laying down detailed rules for the refund of value added tax, provided for in Directive 77/388/EEC, to taxable persons not established in the territory of the country but established in another Member State (A6-0324/2005) Raporteur: Zsolt Laszlo BECSEY (EPP-ED, HU)
Report adopted by 512 votes in favour, 81 against with 10 abstentions.
European regulatory agencies (B6-0634/2005)
Resolution by the Constitutional Affairs and the Budgets Committees adopted by raise of hands.
Preparation for the WTO conference (RC6-0619/2005)
Joint resolution by the political groups adopted by 475 votes in favour to 106 against with 14 abstentions..
Human rights in Cambodia, Laos and Vietnam (RC6-0622/2005)
Joint resolution by the political groups adopted by raise of hands.
Truce during Turin Winter Olympics 2006 (B6-0618/2005)
Resolution by the Culture Committee adopted by raise of hands.
Development and Sport (B6-0633/2005)
Joint resolution by the political groups adopted by raise of hands.
Approval of the Commission Own-initiative report on guidelines for the approval of the European Commission (A6-0179/2005) Rapporteur: Andrew DUFF (ALDE, UK)
Report adopted by raise of hands.
Application of competition rules to maritime transport Own-initiative report on the application of EC competition rules to maritime transport (A6-314/2005) Rapporteur: Rodi KRATSA-TSAGAROPOULOU (EPP-ED, EL)
Report adopted with a number of amendments by 408 votes in favour to 139 against with 62 abstentions.
Electronic communications Communication from the Commission to the Council, the European Parliament, the European Economic and Social Committee and the Committee of the Regions: European Electronic Communications Regulation and Markets 2004 (A6-0305/2005) Rapporteur: Patrizia TOIA (ALDE, IT)
Report adopted by raise of hands.
VAT on highly labour-intensive services (B6-0630/2005) Joint resolution by the political groups adopted by raise of hands.
