Parliament to host youth volunteering convention
Youth
General
2011-09-05 - 14:38
Andreas KLEINER EP Press Service PORT (+32) 498 98 33 22 EMAIL andreas.kleiner@europarl.europa.eu Letizia Gambini European Youth Forum Communications Coordinator PORT +32 491 04 13 78 EMAIL letizia.gambini@youthforum.org The European Parliament is proud to support the European Youth Forum in organising its second Youth Convention on Volunteering, which will take place in Brussels from 7 to 11 September 2011 under the title “Young Volunteers Stand Up!”. Set to attract around 1000 young people from 27 European countries and beyond, the Convention will be the biggest civil-society event during the 2011 European Year of Volunteering.
A press conference will be held on Wednesday 7 September at 3.30 p.m. in the European Parliament press conference room (Anna Politkovskaya, PHSA050) to present the activity-rich programme of the Convention. Speakers will include:
- Mr Gianni Pittella, MEP and Vice-President of the European Parliament
- Ms Katarina Nevedalova, MEP and Vice-President of the European Youth Forum
- Mr Giuseppe Porcaro, Secretary General of the European Youth Forum
- Mr Staffan Nillsson, President of the European Economic and Social Committee
- Mrs Marian Harkin, MEP
Amongst many other events, 80 youth organisations and partners will build a Volunteering Village on the Esplanade of the European Parliament. The official Village opening will take place on 8 September at 6.00 p.m. in presence of Her Royal Highness Princess Mathilde of Belgium and Ms Isabelle Durant, MEP and EP Vice-President.
The programme will feature a Stakeholder Conference, which will discuss a Charter on the Rights of the Volunteer, interactive debates, workshops and trainings on volunteering. There will also be a free music festival ("YO! Fest"), featuring Hot Chip DJ Set – Al Doyle, Motel Connection, Ojos de Brujo, La Chiva Gantiva and other bands.
Interviews will be possible. Please contact Andreas.Kleiner@europarl.europa.eu for more information.
