Reportage - Belgians and expatriates voting
2009 elections
Institutions
2009-06-07 - 20:37
The Italian Embassy in Brussels. Europeans can vote either in another country or at the Embassy if they live abroad. Date stamps are crucial to seeing when people vote. Election officials at the Italian Embassy verify the voter's identity. A ballot box A contented voter at the Italian Embassy. A Belgian voting in Brussels - the card is put into the electronic machine. An estimated 376 million people are entitled to vote in the European elections. Over the weekend we sent out our photographer to collect images of people voting. He came back with images of Belgian citizens casting their vote and also of some Italians living in Brussels voting. Have a look what he found when he was out and about.
