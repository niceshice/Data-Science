The Week Ahead 25-31 May 2009
2009-05-20 - 14:34
Campaign for 4-7 June European Elections
Election campaign. This week is set aside for MEPs to join their political parties and other candidates in the campaign for support in the European Parliament elections of 4-7 June 2009. There are therefore no official meetings planned in Brussels.
