Parliament elects Anni Podimata and Giles Chichester as Vice-Presidents
Institutions
Plenary sittings
2011-07-05 - 12:31
Anni Podimata (S&D, EL) and Giles Chichester (ECR, UK) were elected today by acclamation as Vice-Presidents of the European Parliament to replace Stavros Lambrinidis, who has become foreign minister of Greece, and Silvana Koch-Mehrin, who resigned as Vice-President.
Mrs Podimata and Mr Chichester will be, respectively, the third and fourteenth vice-presidents and will take on the portfolios of their predecessors.
