2009-07-16 - 13:12
A list of British and Irish MEPs appointments to the 20 EP Committee and 2 sub-Committees. Sharon BOWLES (ALDE, UK), Malcolm HARBOUR (ECR, UK) and Brian SIMPSON (S&amp;D, UK) have been appointed chairs of the Economic and Monetary Affairs Committee and the Internal Market and Consumer Protection Committee and Transport and Tourism Committee respectively.
