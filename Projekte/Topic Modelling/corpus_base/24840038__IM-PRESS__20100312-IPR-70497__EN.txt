Women's rights: how to end violence against women
Women's rights/Equal opportunities
2010-03-12 - 10:24
A seminar for journalists on ways to combat violence against women, and EU legal instruments for this purpose, will be held in Parliament on Monday 15 March from 14.45h to 18.00h in room PHS 7C050, Brussels.
Chaired by Silvana Koch-Mehrin (ALDE, DE), EP Vice-President and President of the High Level Group on Gender Equality and Diversity, the seminar will be an opportunity for journalists to discuss this issue with MEPs and the EU's Spanish presidency. Speakers will include, Women's Rights and Gender Equality Committee chair Eva Britt Svensson, Spain's Minister for Equality Bibiana Aído Almagro, and the following guest speakers:
Miguel Lorente, Delegate on Gender Violence, Spanish Presidency
Juan Fernando López Aguilar, Chair of Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs Committee
Edit Bauer MEP, rapporteur on combating human trafficking and protecting victims
Carmen Romero López MEP, rapporteur on the European Protection Warrant
Teresa Jiménez-Becerril Barrio MEP, rapporteur on the European Protection Order
Chantal Louis, Editor at EMMA, Germany
Joan Smith, Writer and Human Rights Activist, United Kingdom
On 16 March, Women's Rights and Gender Equality Committee will hold a public hearing on this theme in the context of International Women's Day 2010.
