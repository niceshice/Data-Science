External Parliamentary Activities Week
External Activities. This week is set aside for MEPs to work in their constituencies or to take part in working visits of parliamentary delegations. These include interparliamentary meetings in Israel, Mauritania, Moldova and the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia. A delegation from the Industry Committee will meet Spanish ministers in Madrid, discuss telecoms policy with Spanish official in Barcelona and visit a solar power plant in Seville. MEPs will also be assessing environmental damage near Huelva in Spain, and the situation in Rosarno, Italy, after recent violent clashes between local residents and immigrants.
There are no official meetings of parliamentary bodies in Brussels this week.
