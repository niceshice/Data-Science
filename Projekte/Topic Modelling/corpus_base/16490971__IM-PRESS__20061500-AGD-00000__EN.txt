The Week Ahead 10 - 16 April 2006
2006-05-10 - 14:50
Constituency Week
Official visit to Croatia and FYROM. President Josep Borrell makes a four-day visit to Croatia and the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia, where he will meet the presidents and prime ministers of both countries. (Monday to Thursday).
Fourth citizens' forum. As part of the debate on the future of Europe and the period of reflection following the rejection of the constitution in France and the Netherlands, the European Parliament is holding its fourth citizens' forum in Lublin, Poland (Monday) .
Parliament's offices will be closed from Thursday 13 April to Monday 17 April. Work resumes after the Easter break on 18 April.

10:30 12:45 Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia Other events 4th Citizens' Forum - The Lublin region in the European Union: challenges and opportunities As part of its contribution to the debate on the future of Europe and the period of reflection following the 'no' votes on the constitution in France and the Netherlands, the European Parliament is organising a series of citizen's forums to enable members of the public directly to enter the debate on the main issues. The fourth of these will take place in Lublin in Poland on 10 April, with a focus on the Lublin region in the EU: challenges and opportunities.
