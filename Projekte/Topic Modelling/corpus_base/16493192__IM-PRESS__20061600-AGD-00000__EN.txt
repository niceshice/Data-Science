The Week Ahead 17 to 23 April 2006
2006-05-16 - 17:28
Committee week - Brussels
Campaign against anti-personnel mines. The EP is staging an event to raise awareness of the problem of anti-personnel mines in Colombia, ahead of the EU-Latin America and Caribbean summit of 11-13 May in Vienna. The Colombian singer Juanes gives a free concert on 19 April in Parliament's plenary chamber. This will be preceded by a press conference with EP President Josep Borrell and Spanish MEP José Ignacio Salafranca (Wednesday) .
ECB. The Economic and Monetary Affairs Committee holds a hearing with Jürgen Starck, who has been proposed as member of the Executive Board of the European Central Bank (Tuesday) .
The EU and the Arab world. The new challenges and opportunities of relations between Europe and the Arab world will be examined at a public hearing (Thursday) .
European energy markets. The actions of regulators and the individual cases of the gas and electricity markets will come under the spotlight at a hearing on Europe's energy markets (Thursday) .
CIA. The temporary committee on alleged kidnappings by the CIA continues its work by hearing evidence from several individuals, including EU anti-terrorism coordinator Gijs De Vries (Thursday) .
