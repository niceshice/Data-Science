2009-06-26 - 10:17
Political groups week – Brussels
Preparing for the new Parliament. The process of reorganisation of political groups in the light of the election results and the new requirement for a group to be made up of at least 25 members from at least seven countries continues in the first of two weeks devoted to preparations for the 14-16 July inaugural plenary session. EP/Greece. EP President Hans-Gert Pöttering will meet Greek President Karolos Papoulias, Prime Minister Kostas Karamanlis and the Speaker of the Hellenic Parliament, Dimitris Sioufas, during a visit to Athens (Tuesday-Thursday). Euro-Med meeting. President Pöttering will also be taking part in a meeting of the Bureau of the Euro-Mediterranean Parliamentary Assembly in Amman, Jordan (Saturday-Sunday).
