2007-09-28 - 08:54
Committee Week - Brussels
Climate change. An interparliamentary meeting is being held jointly by the European Parliament and the Portuguese parliament to contribute to the debate on climate change. EP President Hans-Gert Pöttering and his counterpart from the Portuguese Assembly of the Republic, Jaime Gama, will hold a press conference ahead of this meeting (Monday and Tuesday, press conference Monday) . Composition of the EP. The Constitutional Affairs Committee will adopt draft treaty amendments relating to the composition of the European Parliament (Tuesday) . IGC. European Commission President José Manuel Barroso takes part in an extraordinary meeting of the Conference of Presidents (the EP's political group leaders) to review the preparatory work for the IGC (Tuesday) . 2008 budget. The EP's position on next year's budget will take shape when the Budgets Committee votes on the first-reading amendments (Monday-Wednesday) . Iraq. MEPs will hold talks with their counterparts from the Iraqi parliament, led by the parliamentary speaker, at a meeting of the Foreign Affairs Committee (Tuesday) . Turkey. The Foreign Affairs Committee votes on a draft resolution ahead of the unveiling by the Commission of its progress report on Turkey's accession to the EU (Tuesday) . Middle East. The chair of the Knesset's foreign affairs and defence committee will be a guest at a meeting of the Subcommittee on Security and Defence (Tuesday) . CFSP. Javier Solana, high representative for the EU's common foreign and security policy, apears before the Foreign Affairs Committee (Wednesday) . Kinnock reforms. The Budgetary Control Committee holds a two-day hearing to assess the impact of the administrative reforms introduced by the European Commission (Tuesday-Wednesday) . EP Cinema Prize. The procedure for choosing the first winner of the EP's Prix Lux for European cinema begins with a press conference by Vice-President Gérard Onesta (Monday) .
