The Week Ahead 17 October– 23 October 2011
2011-10-14 - 11:35
Committee meetings and political groups' week
Long-term EU budget. MEPs discuss spending priorities, future own resources and how to make the best use of every euro spent, with national MPs, the Polish Presidency and the Commission in a conference on the EU long-term budget for 2014-2020 (Thursday-Friday) .
Elections in Tunisia. A EP delegation travels to Tunisia to observe the constituent assembly elections, as part of the EU's overall Election Observation Mission for the first of the Arab Spring elections (Wednesday-Tuesday) .
Economic governance. The Economic and Monetary Affairs Committee holds a public hearing on economic governance and crisis management, with speakers including Jürgen Stark, former Member of the ECB Executive Board (Monday) .
Sakharov Prize. A shortlist of three nominees for the 2011 Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought will be selected by the Foreign Affairs and Development committees (Thursday) . The winner will be chosen by the political group leaders at the October plenary session.
Galileo. Parliament and the Commission will mark the launch of the first two operational Galileo satellites from Kourou, French Guiana, with an event on the "Solidarność 1980 Esplanade". The launch will be retransmitted live (Thursday).
Journalism Prize. President Buzek will present awards in the written press, radio, TV and internet categories at this year’s EP Prize for Journalism (Wednesday).
President's diary. Jerzy Buzek meets Marian Lupu, speaker of the Moldovan Parliament on Tuesday. He will be in Frankfurt for the farewell of Jean-Claude Trichet on Wednesday. On Thursday, he meets Uruguay's Presiednt José Mujica, in Brussels. On Sunday, he addresses the meeting of EU leaders before holding a press conference (Justus Lipsius building) .
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament’s political groups will prepare the 24-27 October plenary session in Strasbourg. There will be votes on the EU budget 2012, legislation to combat child pornography, a ban on dishwasher detergent phosphates and the new deal with Australia on air passenger data transfers.
Pre-session press briefing. The regular press briefing by the EP Press Service and political group spokespeople is on Friday at 11.00h (Anna Politkovskaya room PHS 0A050) .
