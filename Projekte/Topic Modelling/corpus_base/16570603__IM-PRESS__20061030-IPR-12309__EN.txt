President Borrell congratulates the President of Bulgaria, Georgi Parvanov on his re-election
Enlargement
2006-10-30 - 16:23
EP President Borrell has congratulated President Parvanov on his 'resounding win, which indicates how much Bulgaria is committed to becoming part of the European family.
It constitutes an unequivocal demonstration of the Bulgarian people's support for the 1 January 2007 accession. I wish President Parvanov every success in his presidential duties'.
