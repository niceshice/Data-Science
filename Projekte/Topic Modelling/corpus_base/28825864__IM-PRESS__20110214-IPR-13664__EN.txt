Barroso to present Commission opinion on Treaty change
Treaties
Plenary sittings
2011-02-15 - 10:06
MEPs will hear and debate a statement of Commission President Jos√© Manuel Barroso on the Treaty change to allow the establishment of a permanent crisis mechanism for the eurozone, as proposed by the Member States. Before such a change can take place, Parliament, the Commission and the European Central Bank will give their opinions. MEPs will debate the Commission opinion with Barroso on Wednesday at 3pm.
Parliament is preparing its own opinion on the issue under the guidance of Elmar Brok (EPP, DE) and Roberto Gualtieri (S&D, IT). Their draft text, to be presented in the Constitutional Affairs Committee on Thursday 17 February, is expected to adopted in March.
