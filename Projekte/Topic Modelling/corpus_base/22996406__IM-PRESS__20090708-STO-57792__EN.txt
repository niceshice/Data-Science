Lighter moments
Institutions
2009-08-13 - 09:02
Some lighter moment of the past 5 years Parliament celebrated its fiftieth anniversary in March 2008. Here some of Europe's younger citizens present their vision of Parliament to the President. How to double the capacity of a Parliament meeting room in one easy lesson Old-fashioned courtesies live on in the European Parliament Whatever the issue was, it had something to do with bananas. Sometimes getting face-to-face with your interviewee requires some improvisation Camera shy? Flamenco dancer Joaquin Cortes, came to Parliament to promote the rights of Roma people. So he had to have his photo taken. What did he say? Britain's Prince Charles throws a quizzical look at President Pöttering during his visit to promote action on the environment. One of a group of visitors to Parliament dressed in medieval costume gets closer to the camera. A Parliament is a place of serious business. Laws are debated and passed, political ideologies clash, world affairs are discussed… But a Parliament is also a place made up by human beings, able also to laugh, enjoy, celebrate and improvise.
These are just a few moments caught by our camera during the last mandate.
