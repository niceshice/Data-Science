The Week Ahead - 22 - 28 May 2006
Institutions
2006-05-22 - 17:33
Constituency Week
Citizens' Forum. The latest in the series of citizens' forums on the future of Europe takes place on Tuesday in Frankfurt-on-the-Oder in Germany, with EP President Josep Borrell among the participants. Borrell in Estonia. President Borrell begins a three-day official visit to Estonia on Sunday. Elections in Colombia. A delegation of MEPs will be observing Sunday's elections in Colombia. MEPs in their constituencies. The week beginning 22 May is set aside for MEPs to work in their constituencies, so there are no official meetings of parliamentary bodies in Brussels. Parliament's offices will be closed on Thursday and Friday for the Ascension holiday.
