Monday, 22 September, an MEP consults the agenda as parliament opens President Pöttering leads tributes the victims of an ETA attack MEPs in the Chamber in Brussels MEPs point the legislative way for Europe MEPs feel public and media scrutiny from the gallery Ecumenical Patriarch Bartholomew called for peace between religions Penguins were used by an environmental group to make their point A regional take on the European Parliament The week also saw a photographic tribute to deceased MEP Bronisław Geremek EU law is signed by the EP President and a representative of the Council MEPs gathered in Brussels on 22 September for the second full parliamentary sitting after the summer recess.
