MEPs divided over the future of nuclear energy in Europe
Energy
Plenary sittings
2011-04-07 - 13:21
Parliament rejected a resolution on nuclear safety in Europe by 264 votes in favour, 300 against and 61 abstentions.
There were various points of disagreement between political groups, which in the end led to a majority rejecting the final text. MEPs debated all these issues on Wednesday 6 April.
