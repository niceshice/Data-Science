Events during the summer recess
2008-07-25 - 09:31
Parliament is in recess. Business resumes on Monday 25 August with a week of meetings of committees and political groups ahead of the plenary session beginning in Strasbourg on 1 September.
EP/Syria and Lebanon. EP President Hans-Gert Pöttering will make an official visit to Syria and Lebanon from 6 to 12 August. See the President's website for further information and press contacts.
EP Press Service contacts. General press enquires can be directed to Hélène Cuisinier (28 July-8 August – tel. +32 2 283 2692, helene.cuisinier@europarl.europa.eu ) and Jean-Yves Loog (11-22 August – tel. +32 284 4652, jean-yves.loog@europarl.europa.eu ).
