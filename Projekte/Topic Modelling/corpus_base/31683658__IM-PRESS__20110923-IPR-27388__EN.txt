Body scanners: images too revealing?
Transport
Plenary sittings
2011-09-28 - 17:00
Michaela FINDEIS BXL (+32) 2 28 31141 STR (+33) 3 881 72076 PORT (+32) 498 98 33 32 EMAIL tran-press@europarl.europa.eu The new technical criteria that body scanners will have to meet to be authorised in European airports will be debated in plenary late on Thursday morning, following a statement by the Commission.
MEPs from various groups are concerned that images may be too detailed, affording too little protection for personal privacy.
