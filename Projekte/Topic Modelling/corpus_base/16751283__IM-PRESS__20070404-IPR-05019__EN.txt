The Week Ahead 9-15 April 2007
Institutions
2007-04-04 - 10:14
Committee Week, Brussels
Roaming. The Industry Committee will vote on the Commission’s plans to cap charges for mobile phone users making calls when visiting another EU country (Thursday). Racism and Xenophobia. MEPs in the Civil Liberties Committee are due to vote on progress made in agreeing an EU-wide decision on combating racism and xenophobia (Wednesday). Food additives. Two first reading reports on food additives are due to be adopted by the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Food Safety (Wednesday). Cat and dog fur. Legislation banning the marketing and import of cat and dog fur will be put to the vote in the Internal Market Committee (Thursday). EP/Netherlands. Parliament President Hans-Gert Pöttering will make an official visit to the Netherlands. He will meet Prime Minister Balkenende and national parliamentary leaders, among others. (Thursday) Lebanon. Following an earlier visit to Israel and the Palestinian territories, a delegation of MEPs will complete its mission examining the aftermath of last summer’s war in Lebanon by travelling to Beirut for high level meetings (Thursday to Sunday). Foreign Affairs. External relations Commissioner Benita Ferrero-Waldner will appear before the Foreign Affairs Committee (Wednesday), which will also be hearing from the German Defence Minister, Franz Josef Jung, on behalf of the Council Presidency (Thursday). Constitution. MEPs in the Constitutional Affairs Committee will debate ratification procedures concerning the Constitutional Treaty with the Netherlands’ European Affairs Minister, Frans Timmermans (Tuesday). Energy Globe Award. Parliament is hosting the 2007 Energy Globe Award ceremony, with President Pöttering joining actor Martin Sheen, UN Good Will Ambassador Waris Dirie, former Environment Minister of India Maneka Gandhi and Bee Gees musician Robin Gibb to present the major environmental prizes. The Industry Committee is hosting a round table discussion on energy issues as part of the event (Wednesday and Thursday).
