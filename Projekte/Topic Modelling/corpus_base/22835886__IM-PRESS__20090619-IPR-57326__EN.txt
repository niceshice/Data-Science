The Week Ahead 22-28 June 2009
Institutions
2009-06-19 - 14:11
Transition to the new Parliament
Budget preparations. The outgoing Budgets Committee meets to prepare for a round of negotiations with the Council on 7 and 10 July on the 2010 EU budget (Tuesday).
Political groups. Informal meetings will continue among newly elected MEPs on the reorganisation of political groups in the light of the election results and the new requirement that a group needs at least 25 MEPs from at least seven countries.
