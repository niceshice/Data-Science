Christmas and New Year Recess
2011-01-01 - 10:04
The European Parliament is in recess over the Christmas and New Year holidays.
Parliamentary business resumes on 10 January with a week of meetings of parliamentary committees and political groups in preparation for the 17-20 January plenary session.
Parliament's Press Service wishes its readers a Happy New Year.
