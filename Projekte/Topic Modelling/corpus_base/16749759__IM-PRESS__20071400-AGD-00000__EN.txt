The Week Ahead 2-8 April 2007
2007-03-30 - 09:24
Constituency week &amp; Easter Holiday
Constituency week. The week beginning 2 April is set aside for MEPs to work in their constituencies, so there are no official meetings in Brussels. Easter Holiday. Parliament's offices are closed from 5-9 April inclusive for the Easter holiday. The next edition of The Agenda will be published on Wednesday 4 April.
