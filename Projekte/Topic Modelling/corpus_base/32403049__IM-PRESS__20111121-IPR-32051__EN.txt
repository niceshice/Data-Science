MEPs react to the Commission's proposals on eurobonds
Economic and monetary affairs
Committees Economic and Monetary Affairs
2011-11-22 - 09:41
Dorota KOLINSKA BXL (+32) 2 28 32787 STR (+33) 3 881 74005 PORT (+32) 498 98 32 80 EMAIL econ-press@europarl.europa.eu On Tuesday and Wednesday, the Economic and Monetary Affairs Committee will meet Commissioners Rehn, Barnier, Šemeta and Almunia, to discuss the eurozone crisis, in particular eurobonds, credit rating agencies and a financial transaction tax. MEPs will be able to react directly to the Commission's ideas on eurobonds, to be unveiled Wednesday morning.
The exact timing is as follows:
22 November 2011
9.00 – 10.30 : Exchange of views with Joaquín Almunia , Commissioner for Competition.
15.00 – 17.00 : Exchange of views with Michel Barnier , Commissioner for the Internal Market and Services
23 November 2011
16.00 – 17.30 : Exchange of views with Olli Rehn , Commissioner for Economic and Monetary Affairs
17.30 – 18.30 Exchange of views with Algirdas Šemeta , Commissioner for Taxation and Customs Union, Audit and Anti-Fraud
