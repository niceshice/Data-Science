2006-10-06 - 10:05
Plenary session and committee meetings - Brussels
Lahti summit. The Brussels plenary will open with a debate with Council and Commission on the forthcoming informal meeting of EU leaders in Lahti on 20 October. (Wednesday) Trade - shoe imports. The plenary will debate the Council's recent extension of anti-dumping duties on shoes from China and Vietnam, before considering a report on wider issues concerning trade defence instruments. (debate Wednesday, vote Thursday) Fundamental rights agency. The creation of an EU Fundamental Rights Agency will also be in the session's spotlight as MEPs debate two reports on the subject. (debate and vote Thursday) REACH. The Environment Committee will hold its second reading vote on legislation for the registration, evaluation and authorisation of chemicals. (Tuesday) Trichet - ECB. European Central Bank President Jean-Claude Trichet appears before the Economic and Monetary Affairs Committee for his regular monetary dialogue with MEPs. The committee will also vote on a report on ECB policy. (Tuesday) Budget 2007. The marathon voting session in the Budgets Committee on the EU's 2007 budget should be completed. (Tuesday and Thursday) EP/Iran meeting. The delegation for relations with Iran will have its first formal meeting with its counterpart from the Majlis, which will be followed by a press conference.
