Food aid for the needy - debate at 15.00h
Agriculture
Plenary sittings
2012-02-14 - 09:39
Ján JAKUBOV BXL (+32) 2 28 32119 STR (+33) 3 881 73840 PORT (+32) 498 98 35 90 EMAIL agri-press@europarl.europa.eu A plan to save the food aid programme for needy EU citizens will be debated on Tuesday from 15.00h. If MEPs endorse the compromise on Wednesday, the programme will run until the end of 2013 with an annual budget ceiling of €500 million.
Rapporteur Czesław Adam Siekierski (EPP, PL) and Agriculture and Rural Development Committee chair Paolo De Castro (S&D, IT), will give a press conference on Wednesday at 15.00h
