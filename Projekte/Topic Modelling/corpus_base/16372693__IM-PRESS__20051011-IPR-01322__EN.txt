EP President Borrell invites Angela Merkel to visit the European Parliament
Constitutional affairs
2005-10-11 - 17:57
Josep Borrell congratulated Angela Merkel on her nomination as the head of the German government. He noted that she would be the first female Chancellor of Germany.
In a press release, the European Parliament President said that when Mrs Merkel and the members of her government have officially been voted into office by the Bundestag, the EP will invite her to communicate and discuss her government's approach to Europe with Members of the European Parliament. The invitation will follow the lines of the major discussions with heads of government of EU Member States, as decided on by the Conference of Political Group Chairmen
