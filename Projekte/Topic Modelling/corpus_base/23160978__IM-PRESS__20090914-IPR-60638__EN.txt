Eighteen chairs of parliamentary delegations elected in Strasbourg
Delegations
2009-09-17 - 14:55
Members of the EP delegations through which MEPs conduct regular dialogues with national and regional parliaments of non-EU countries began electing their chairs and vice-chairs in Strasbourg this week, with eighteen of the forty parliamentary delegations holding their constituent meetings on Wednesday 16 and Thursday 17 September.
The following delegations elected their officers in Strasbourg:
