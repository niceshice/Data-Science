EP president Schulz to address Greek parliament
Institutions
Economic and monetary affairs
2012-02-28 - 14:45
Greek parliament EP president Martin Schulz will express the European Parliament's solidarity with the Greek people when he addresses the national parliament this evening. Today he is on his first official visit to Athens, where he will meet the country's political leaders. The parliamentary meeting and debate he will be taking part in will be streamed live on our website from 1800 CET.
Mr Schulz will be meeting president Karolos Papoulias, prime minister Loukas Papademos and the leaders of the political parties participating in the coalition government. This evening he will address a joint session of the European, economics, foreign and defence affairs committees, which will be followed by an exchange of views.
