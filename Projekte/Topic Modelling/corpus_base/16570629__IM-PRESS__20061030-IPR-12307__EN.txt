President Borrell congratulates Lula on his re-election
External relations
2006-10-30 - 16:49
On the occasion of Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva’s re-election as President of the Republic of Brazil, President Borrell congratulated him ‘on the deep trust the people of Brazil have shown in him. This victory is an endorsement for him to press ahead with the process of social reform he began during his first term of office’.
Mr Borrell repeated the invitation he extended in August 2005 for President Lula to visit a plenary part-session at the European Parliament.
President Borrell wished President Lula all the best in carrying out his duties.
