Institutions
Ireland
2009-07-20 - 14:30
Below is a list of the 12 Irish Members and their appointments to parliamentary committees. In addition, Jim Higgins (EPP, Fine Gael, Ireland) was elected one of the five Quaestors on 15 July in Strasbourg for a two and a half year term. The Quaestors are responsible for administrative and financial matters of direct concern to Members. The five Quaestors sit on Parliament's Bureau in an advisory capacity.
