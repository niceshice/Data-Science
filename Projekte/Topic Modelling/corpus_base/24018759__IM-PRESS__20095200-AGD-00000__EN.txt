The Week Ahead 21-27 December 2009
2009-12-17 - 16:29
Parliament is in recess for the Christmas and New Year holidays.
EP President visits Ukraine. The President of the European Parliament, Jerzy Buzek, will make an official visit to Ukraine. His schedule includes meetings with political leaders, senior officials and others (Monday-Tuesday).
Christmas/New Year recess. The European Parliament is in recess over the Christmas and New Year holidays. Parliament's offices are closed from 23 December 2009 to 3 January 2010 inclusive. Parliamentary business resumes on 11 January with a week devoted to hearings of Commissioners-designate and meetings of political groups ahead of the 18-22 January plenary session.
Urgent press enquiries during the holiday period should be addressed to:
23-27 December: Malene Chaucheprat on +32 498 983 325
28 December - 3 January: Marjory van den Broeke on +32 498 983 586
Parliament's Press Service wishes its readers a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.
