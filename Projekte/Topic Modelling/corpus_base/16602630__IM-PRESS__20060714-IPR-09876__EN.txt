The Week Ahead 17-23 July 2006
Institutions
2006-07-18 - 17:04
Parliament in recess
Conference of Presidents. The leaders of Parliament's political groups will hold a special meeting on Thursday (11am-1pm) to discuss developments in the Middle East. This will be followed by an informal press briefing by EP President Josep Borrell outside the meeting room (PHS 6B054).
Press enquires: Jaume Duch, European Parliament Spokesman, tel: +32 2 28 43000
President of Mozambique. President Borrell will welcome the President of Mozambique, Armando Guebuza, to the European Parliament at 11am on Wednesday.
EP delegation in Nepal. An ad-hoc delegation of seven MEPs from the Development Committee and the South Asia/SAARC Delegation, led by Neena Gill (PES, UK), will be in Nepal until Thursday to assess the situation following the recent political turmoil.
Press enquires: Thomas Dudrap, tel + 32 498 98 32 84
Advance notice - electoral observation in Congo. A delegation of MEPs will be travelling to the Democratic Republic of the Congo to observe the presidential and parliamentary elections of 30 July.
Press officer travelling with the delegation: Marjory van den Broeke, tel +32 498 983 586
Summer recess. The parliamentary recess begins on 17 July 2006. Official business resumes on 28 August.
