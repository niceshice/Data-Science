Photos and names of committee chairs Last week all of parliament's committees met in Brussels to elect chairs and vice-chairs for the coming two and a half years until the end of the current legislature in June 2014.
Parliament's legislative work is carried out by 20 standing committees and two sub-committees, each of which elects a chair and four vice-chairs. Check out who's who in the EP's committees.
