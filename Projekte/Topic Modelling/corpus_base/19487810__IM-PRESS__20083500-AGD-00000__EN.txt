The Week Ahead 25-31 August 2008
2008-08-25 - 15:01
Committee meetings and political groups week - Brussels
Resumption of parliamentary business. Meetings take place in Brussels to prepare for the September I plenary session of the following week which will, exceptionally, take place in Brussels.
The final order of business for the session will be confirmed by the Conference of Presidents on Thursday 28 August.
Parliament's press service and spokespersons of the political groups will hold a press briefing on the September I session on Friday 29 August.
Human Rights. Among the committee meetings taking place, the sub-committee on Human Rights will hold a series of exchanges of views on current issues including China, Georgia and Vietnam.
