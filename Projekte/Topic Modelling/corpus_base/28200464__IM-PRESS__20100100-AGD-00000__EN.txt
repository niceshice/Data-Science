Christmas and New Year Recess
2010-12-24 - 10:05
The European Parliament is in recess over the Christmas and New Year holidays.
Parliament's offices are closed from 21 December to 2 January inclusive. Parliamentary business resumes on 10 January with a week of meetings of parliamentary committees and political groups in preparation for the 17-20 January plenary session.
Urgent press enquiries during the holiday period until 2 January should be addressed to:
Marjory van den Broeke (Head of the Press Unit) on +32 498 983 586
Parliament's Press Service wishes its readers a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.
