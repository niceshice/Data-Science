MEPs call for unified EU response to debt crisis
Economic and monetary affairs
Plenary sittings
2011-09-14 - 13:42
John SCHRANZ BXL (+32) 2 28 44264 STR (+33) 3 881 74076 PORT (+32) 498 98 14 02 EMAIL econ-press@europarl.europa.eu With the economic crisis in the European Union showing no signs of abating, MEPs Wednesday emphasised the need for a unified European response to stabilise the situation. The adoption of a legislative package on economic governance would be a big step in the right direction, MEPs agreed while warning that ordinary people cannot be expected to bear the whole cost of the crisis.
