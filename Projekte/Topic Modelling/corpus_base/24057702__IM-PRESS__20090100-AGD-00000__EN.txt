Christmas and New Year recess
2009-12-25 - 09:03
Parliament is in recess for the Christmas and New Year holidays
The European Parliament is in recess over the Christmas and New Year holidays.
Parliament's offices reopen on 4 January 2010.
Parliamentary business resumes on 11 January with a week devoted to hearings of Commissioners-designate and meetings of political groups ahead of the 18-22 January plenary session.
Urgent press enquiries during the holiday period should be addressed to:
23-27 December: Malene Chaucheprat on +32 498 983 325
28 December - 3 January: Marjory van den Broeke on +32 498 983 586
Parliament's Press Service wishes its readers a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.
