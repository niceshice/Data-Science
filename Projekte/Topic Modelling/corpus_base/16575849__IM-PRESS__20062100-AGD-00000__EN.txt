The Week Ahead - 22 - 28 May 2006
2006-05-19 - 10:52
Constituency Week
Citizens' Forum. The 13th citizens' forums on the future of Europe takes place on Tuesday in Frankfurt-on-the-Oder in Germany, with EP President Josep Borrell among the participants.
Borrell in Estonia. President Borrell begins a three-day official visit to Estonia on Sunday.
Elections in Colombia. A delegation of MEPs will be observing Sunday's elections in Colombia.
MEPs in their constituencies. The week beginning 22 May is set aside for MEPs to work in their constituencies, so there are no official meetings of parliamentary bodies in Brussels. Parliament's offices will be closed on Thursday and Friday for the Ascension holiday.
2006-05-23 The President's diary - Participation in the Citizens' Forum on "Europe's aims - Europe's borders", European University Viadrina 18:00 20:00 Frankfurt-Oder (Germany) Other events 13th Citizens' Forum: "Europe's aims - Europe's borders" As part of its contribution to the debate on the future of Europe and the period of reflection following the 'no' votes on the constitution in France and the Netherlands, the European Parliament is organising a series of citizen's forums to enable members of the public directly to enter the debate on the main issues. This 13th Citizens' Forum will focus on EU enlargement related issues and includes EP President Josep Borrell among the participants.
