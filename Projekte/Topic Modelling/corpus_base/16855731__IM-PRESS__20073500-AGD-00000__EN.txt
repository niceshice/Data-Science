The Week Ahead 27 August – 2 September 2007
2007-08-24 - 11:45
Committee meetings and political groups week - Brussels
Parliamentary business resumes. Following the summer break, MEPs return to Brussels for a series of committee meetings and the usual meetings of political groups to prepare for the Strasbourg plenary session of 3-6 September. Though changes are possible, the main topics of the draft agenda include the counter terrorism measures, natural disasters, transport of dangerous goods, the issue of liquids on board airliners and controls on the possession and purchase of weapons.
Racism. The Fundamental Rights Agency will present a report on tackling racism and xenophobia to the Civil Liberties Committee. (Monday)
Human Rights. A mini-hearing by the Human Rights Subcommittee will study the human rights situation in the EU’s neighbouring countries, focussing on Jordan and Azerbaijan as examples. (Monday)
Pre-session press briefing. The EP Press Service and political group spokespeople will be on hand for their regular press briefing ahead of the September I plenary session at 11am on Friday 31 August (room PHS 0A050).
