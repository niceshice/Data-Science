The Week Ahead - 18-24 December 2006
2006-12-15 - 09:55
Committee week - Brussels
Extraordinary plenary session. The Finnish Council Presidency reports to a special plenary session of the EP on the European Council meeting of 14 and 15 December. Before the start of the session, Parliament and the Council will officially sign the REACH regulation and the 7th framework research programme (Monday) .
Group leaders in Berlin. The political group leaders go to Berlin for a meeting with the Germany Presidency, which chairs the EU from 1 January 2007 (Monday) .
Visit to the Middle East. An EP ad hoc delegation travels to Israel, Gaza and the West Bank for a series of meetings with the Israeli and Palestinian authorities (Tuesday-Thursday).
ECB. European Central Bank President Jean-Claude Trichet appears before the Economic and Monetary Affairs Committee as part of the regular monetary dialogue (Wednesday) .
Railways. Three reports, on certification of train crews, the development of the EU's railways and rights of international passengers, are to be put to the vote at the Transport Committee (Tuesday) .
CIA. The temporary committee investigating illegal activities by the CIA holds a further discussion with Commissioner Frattini (Tuesday) .

The Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs
The Civil Liberties Committee is to hold a seminar on "Efficient and accountable police cooperation in the EU - The way forward". The aim is to create a constructive forum on how to improve police cooperation while protecting the rights of the individuals concerned and furthering democratic control. Issues such as data protection standards, current obstacles to improving police cooperation and how to improve parliamentary control over it will be raised.
Among the main guests there will be Vice-President of the European Commission Franco FRATTINI, Director of the Operational Police Support at Interpol Jean Michel LOUBOUTIN, Deputy Director of Europol Michel QUILLE, CEPOL Director Ulf GORANSSON and European Data Protection Supervisor Peter HUSTINX 15:00 18:30 Brussels, PHS 5B001 Public Seminar on "An efficient and accountable police cooperation in the EU - the way forward" Other events Inter Parliamentary colloquium with MEPs and MPs of the Western Balkan Countries
