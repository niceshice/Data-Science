Through the lens - 736 MEPs will on 14 July take up their seats for 5 years. Once elected they will sit not as national delegations but as trans-national groups. From the north of Finland to the south of Spain Europeans are going to the polls to elect a new European Parliament. Amid a background of the continent being in economic crisis the outcome is eagerly anticipated. Here in this slideshow we look at some images of people voting and preparations for the results on Sunday 7 June.
