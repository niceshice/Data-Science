Events during the summer recess
Institutions
2006-07-21 - 10:01
Parliament is in recess until official business resumes on 28 August 2006.
Events of media interest over the summer include:
Electoral observation in Congo. A delegation of MEPs will be travelling to the Democratic Republic of the Congo to observe the presidential and parliamentary elections of 30 July.
Press officer travelling with the delegation: Marjory van den Broeke, tel +32 498 983 586
EP President's activity. Josep Borrell has a number of official engagements in the weeks ahead, including:
Slovenia 29-30 July - participation in a ceremony marking 90 years since an avalanche killed Russian prisoners of war at Vršič;
Central and Latin America 7-20 August - a series of working visits;
United Kingdom 23-26 August - participation in the Edinburgh Festival of Politics.
