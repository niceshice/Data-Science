MEPs and MPs discuss "Equal pay for work of equal value"
Women's rights/Equal opportunities
Committees Women's Rights and Gender Equality
2012-03-07 - 10:03
Federico DE GIROLAMO BXL (+32) 2 28 31389 STR (+33) 3 881 72850 PORT (+32) 498 98 35 91 EMAIL femm-press@europarl.europa.eu Parliament will mark International Women's Day on 8 March with a meeting between MEPs and national MPs on "Equal pay for work of equal value", which will assess the impact of measures to reduce the gender pay gap and consider possible further initiatives to tackle its causes. President Martin Schulz will open the debate on Thursday at 9 a.m.
