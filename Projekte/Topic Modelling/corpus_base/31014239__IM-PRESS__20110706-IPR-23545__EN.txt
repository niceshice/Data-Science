EP election reform referred back to committee
Institutions
Plenary sittings
2011-07-07 - 12:38
A vote on the reform of the European Parliament elections was postponed on 7 July, as MEPs decided to send it back to the Constitutional Affairs Committee for further discussion. The main proposal in the resolution, drafted by Andrew Duff (ALDE, UK), is the creation of transnational lists from which citizens could choose 25 MEPs.
Procedure: Non-legislative resolution
