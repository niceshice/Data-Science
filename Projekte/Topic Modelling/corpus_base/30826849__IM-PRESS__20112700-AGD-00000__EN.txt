The Week Ahead 27 June - 3 July 2011
2011-06-24 - 09:37
Committee meetings and political groups week – Brussels
Jean-Claude Trichet. The European Central Bank President will take part in the regular monetary dialogue with the Economics Committee (Thursday).
Herman Van Rompuy. The European Council President will debate the outcome of the EU summit at a meeting of group leaders open to all MEPs. Commission President José Manuel Barroso will also take part. Earlier, EP President Jerzy Buzek has bilateral meetings with both Mr Van Rompuy and Mr Barroso (Tuesday).
Help for dairy farmers The Agriculture Committee will vote on legislation to help dairy farmers in view of the end of milk quotas in 2015 (Monday).
EP President's diary. Jerzy Buzek will be in Warsaw for the opening ceremony of the Polish Council presidency and in Wrocław to open the EP’s new information office (Friday).
Preparing for the plenary. Parliament’s political groups will devote most of the week to preparations for the 4-7 July Strasbourg plenary session. The main items on the agenda are second reading votes on food labelling and cross-border traffic offences, legislation on GMOs, derivatives, short selling and investor compensation, proposals to change the EP electoral system, debates with the Hungarian and Polish Prime Ministers as they respectively complete and begin their Council presidencies, and debates on North Africa and the Arab world, Syria and Yemen with Commission Vice-President/High Representative Catherine Ashton.
Pre-session press briefing. Parliament’s press service and the political group spokespeople will be available at the regular briefing on Friday at 11am (Anna Politkovskaya room PHS 0A050).
