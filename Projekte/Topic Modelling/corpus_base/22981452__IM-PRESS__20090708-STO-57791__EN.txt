VIPs in The European Parliament
Institutions
2009-08-06 - 09:03
Some of the VIPs that pass through the Parliaments corridors in the last 5 years Hillary Clinton Ingrid Betancourt The Dalai Lama Kofi Annan Aliaksandr Milinkevich Mikhail Gorbachev Desmond Tutu Yulia Tymoshenko Carla Del Ponte The European Parliament has long been a magnet for visiting foreign leaders. The past legislative term was no exception, with a succession of well known international figures visiting the Parliament: some to address the Parliament in formal session, others to attend political meetings or special events, others to receive awards.
This selection of nine photographs cannot do justice to the very many eminent guests of Parliament over the last five years, but it can provide an indication of the variety of figures who made the journey to Brussels or Strasbourg to meet MEPs.
