Mahmoud Abbas and EP President Hans-Gert Pöttering Vaclav Klaus Hillary Clinton Mr Pöttering and other standing under a blue screen saying "Earthquake in Italy" A toy seal on a desk BBC reporter Jonathan Charles in front of a screen showing election results Joseph Daul sitting in front of a large screen showing Martin Schulz Jerzy Buzek in the plenary chamber Group of young people with painted faces Mr Barroso and some of the MEPs who asked questions Pieces of the Berlin Wall on display in Brussels An ice sculpture in Strasbourg We began 2009 with conflict in the Middle East and towards the end of the year we marked the 20th anniversary of the fall of the Berlin.
