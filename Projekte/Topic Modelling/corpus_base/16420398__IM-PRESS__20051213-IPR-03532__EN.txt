Institutions

A list of the reports put to the vote in the European Parliament on Thursday 15 December. More detailed analyses of votes will soon be available from Parliament's Press Service website
Results of votes on Thursday 15 December
Budget: mobilisation of flexibility instrument
(A6-0395/2005)
Rapporteur : Reimer Böge (EPP-ED, DE)
Report adopted by raise of hands and by double majority.
Rejection of budget
(B6-0642/2005)
Resolution rejected by 32 votes in favour to 472 against with 10 abstentions.
Budget 2006
Draft general budget 2006, as modified by the Council (all sections)
Parliament adopted a package of amendments, thus approving the agreement which had been reached with the Council earlier. The payment appropriations for 2006 will amount to €119.969 bn (or 1.01% of GNI) and commitment appropriations to €121.189 (or 1.09% of GNI).
