Votes of Tuesday 27 September
Coordination of procedures for the award of public work contracts Rapporteur: Stefano Zappalà (A6-0270/2005) Proposal for a directive correcting Directive 2004/18/EC on the coordination of procedures for the award of public works contracts, public supply contracts and public service contracts [COM(2005)0214 - C6-0155/2005 - 2005/0100(COD)] Report adopted
Civil aviation agreement with Bulgaria Rapporteur : Paolo Costa (A6-0258/2005) Proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of the Agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Bulgaria on certain aspects of air services [COM(2005)0158 - C6-0177/2005 - 2005/0060(CNS)] Report adopted
Civil aviation agreement with Croatia Rapporteur : Paolo Costa (A6-0259/2005) Proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of the Agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Croatia on certain aspects of air services [COM(2005)0159 - C6-0173/2005 - 2005/0059(CNS)] Report adopted
Fees of the European Medicines Agency Rapporteur: Karl-Heinz Florenz (A6-0264/2005) Proposal for a Council regulation amending Regulation (EC) No 297/95 on fees payable to the European Medicines Agency [COM(2005)0106 - C6-0137/2005 - 2005/0023(CNS)] Report adopted
Protocol to EEC-Comores Agreement on tuna fishing Rapporteur: Carmen Fraga Estévez (A6-0260/2005) Proposal for a Council regulation on the conclusion of the Protocol setting out the tuna fishing opportunities and financial contribution provided for in the Agreement between the European Economic Community and the Islamic Federal Republic of the Comoros on fishing off the Comoros for the period from 1 January 2005 to 31 December 2010 [COM(2005)0187 - C6-0154/2005 - 2005/0092(CNS)] Report adopted by 473 votes in favour, 54 against and 82 abstentions.
Waiver of the immunity of Marios Matsakis Rapporteur: Klaus-Heiner Lehne (A6-0268/2005) Request for waiver of the immunity of Mr Marios Matzakis [2004/2194(IMM)] Report adopted, thus waiving Mr Matsakis' immunity.
Retention of data to combat crime, including terrorism Rapporteur: Alexander Nuno Alvaro (A6-0174/2005) Initiative by the French Republic, Ireland, the Kingdom of Sweden and the United Kingdom for a Council framework decision on the retention of data processed and stored in connection with the provision of publicly available electronic communications services or data on public communications networks for the purposes of the prevention, investigation, detection and prosecution of crime and criminal offences including terrorism [8958/2004 - C6-0198/2004 - 2004/0813(CNS)] Report adopted, thus rejecting the initiative of France, the UK, Ireland and Sweden.
Granting and withdrawing refugee status Rapporteur: Wolfgang Kreissl-Dörfler (A6-0222/2005) Amended proposal for a Council directive on minimum standards on procedures in Member States for granting and withdrawing refugee status [14203/2004 - C6-0200/2004 - 2000/0238(CNS)] Amended report adopted by 305 votes in favour, 302 against and 33 abstentions.
