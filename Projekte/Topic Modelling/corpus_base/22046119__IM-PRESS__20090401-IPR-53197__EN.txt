MEPs vote to postpone vote on resolution on Arctic International Treaty
Environment
2009-04-02 - 12:34
At the suggestion of Avril Doyle (EPP-ED, IE) and supported by Diana Wallis (ALDE, UK), MEPs voted to postpone a vote on the resolution on the International Arctic Treaty given the ongoing nature of the negotiations with the Arctic Council.
