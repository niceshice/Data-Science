European court ruling on crucifixes in Italian schools: vote postponed
Fundamental rights
2009-12-17 - 12:51
Parliament decided to postpone its vote on a resolution concerning the recent judgment of the European Court of Human Rights in Strasbourg regarding the display of crucifixes in Italian schools. MEPs held a heated debate on this issue on Tuesday 15 December but decided today to check the admissibility of the vote and the resolution.
