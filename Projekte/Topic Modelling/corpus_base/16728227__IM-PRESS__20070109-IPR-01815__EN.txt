At the midway point of a parliamentary term, MEPs can change committee
At the midway point of each parliamentary term, the composition of the twenty Parliamentary Committees and two sub-Committees can change. Parliament adopted the new composition which was based on a proposal from the Conference of Presidents. Below is a list of the Irish MEP appointments to the various parliamentary committees. Avril DOYLE (EPP/ED was elected 2nd Vice-Chair of the Fisheries Committee and Kathy SINNOTT (IND/DEM) was elected 3rd Vice-Chair of the Petitions Committee.
The constitutive meetings of the Parliamentary Committees took place on the evening of 31 January and the morning of 1 February when the new Chairs and four Vice-Chairs of each committee were elected.
