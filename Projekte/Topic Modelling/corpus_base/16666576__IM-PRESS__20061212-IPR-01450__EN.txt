Bulgarian and Romanian Candidates for the Court of Auditors approved
Budgetary control
2006-12-12 - 13:11
Parliament approved both the Bulgarian and Romanian candidates for the Court of Auditors.
Parliament voted by secret ballot (in accordance with Rule 162) in favour of the nomination of the Bulgarian Member of the Court of Auditors Nadezhda SANDOLOVA as a Member of the Court of Auditors. Parliament voted 561 votes in favour, 32 against with 38 abstentions.
Parliament also voted in favour by secret ballot on the nomination of the Romanian Member of the Court of Auditors - Ovidiu ISPIR as a Member of the Court of Auditors. Parliament adopted the report with 356 votes in favour, 229 against and 51 abstentions.
