The Week Ahead 5 - 11 November 2007
Institutions
2007-10-31 - 10:00
Committee week and political groups - Brussels
Terrorism . The new European anti-terrorism co-ordinator Gilles de Kerchove will hold a first exchange of views with the Civil Liberties Committee, further to his appointment ( Monday ). Doha . Trade Commissioner Peter Mandelson will brief the Development Committee on progress on the Doha agenda ( Monday ). Court of Auditors . One week before the presentation of its 2006 annual report, the Court of Auditors makes a preliminary appearance before the Budgetary Control Committee, for the appointment hearing of the Court's seven members. Climate Change Committee in China . Representatives of the Temporary Committee on Climate Change (CLIM) will go to China, on a preparatory mission for the United Nations conference to be held in Bali in December ( Monday-Wednesday ). Citizens' Agora . The European Parliament will host on its premises the very first "Citizens' Agora", bringing together hundreds of civil society delegates to contribute to debate on Europe's future ( Thursday and Friday ). Preparing the November session. Political groups will prepare for a November plenary session to be marked by the official visit of the President of the French Republic, Nicolas Sarkozy. Soil protection, including aviation in greenhouse gas emission trading schemes and globalisation are among the highlights of the session. The pre-session briefing will be held on Friday at 11.00h.
