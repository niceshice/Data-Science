The Week Ahead: 27 October to 2 November 2008
Institutions
2008-10-24 - 10:48
External parliamentary activities week
South Africa. European Parliament President Hans-Gert Pöttering, visits South Africa for the opening of the 10th ordinary session of the Pan-African Parliament and meetings with various leading figures (Monday-Tuesday). Horn of Africa. A Development Committee delegation is visiting Eritrea, Ethiopia and Djibouti to assess food availability after the world crisis caused by soaring prices, efforts to resolve frontier conflicts and good governance in the region. MEPs are to meet representatives of governments, international organisations and NGOs (25 October to 2 November). President Pöttering will go to Ethiopia, where he will meet, among others, President Girma Woldegiorgis (Friday). Palestinian Territories. Eight MEP's from Parliament's delegation to the Palestinian Legislative Council go to the West Bank and Gaza Strip for the eighth interparliamentary meeting with the Palestinian Legislative Council and to assess the situation on the ground, particularly in Gaza. They will also meet Prime Minister Salam Fayyad, NGOs and representatives of the local population (Friday-Monday). Climate change. MEPs on the EP Temporary Committee will be in Moscow for exchanges with their counterparts in the Russian Duma and the Federation Council of Russia, and representatives of the Russian government and civil society on climate change.
