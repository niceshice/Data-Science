The Week Ahead 24 - 27 August 2009
2009-08-25 - 14:58
Political groups week - Brussels
Parliamentary business resumes on Monday 24 August with a week of meetings of political groups. During the following week, starting on Monday 31, Parliament's committee will hold meetings with ministers from the Swedish Presidency, as well as three hearings of the Budget, Development and Regional policy Commissioners-designate.
