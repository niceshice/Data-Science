MEPs adopt plenary sessions calendar for 2008
Institutions
2007-07-11 - 16:19
The House adopted the following Strasbourg and Brussels plenary sessions for 2008 as set out in the link below. MEPs narrowly voted in favour (331 to 328) of rejecting the idea of introducing an additional eight one-day part sessions in Brussels.
As usual there will be 12 plenary sessions in Strasbourg (two in September) lasting four days and six in Brussels lasting two days.
