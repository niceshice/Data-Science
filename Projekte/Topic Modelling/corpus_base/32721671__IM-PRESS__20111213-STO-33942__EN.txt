Sakharov Prize: follow awards ceremony, chat with winner from Egypt on Facebook
Fundamental rights
2011-12-13 - 13:09
Large Sakharov prize poster suspended above the corridor outside the hemicycle This year's Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought will be awarded on Wednesday to five activists of the Arab uprising. Two of them will be in Parliament to receive the award in person from President Jerzy Buzek. Tune in to see Egypt's Asmaa Mahfouz and Libya's Ahmed el Senussi receive the award at 1200 CET.
Ahead of the awards ceremony, Asmaa Mahfouz, who mobilised Egyptian activists with her Youtube videos, Tweets and posts on Facebook, will answer questions in a chat on the EP's Facebook page. Don't miss the opportunity to chat live with one of the Arab Spring activists from 0945 on Wednesday morning
